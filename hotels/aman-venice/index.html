Aman Venice

A 16th-Century Masterpiece on the Grand Canal

5-Star Aman Collection Grand Canal, Venice
24 Suites
€2,000+ Per Night
16th C. Palazzo Built

Aman Venice occupies Palazzo Papadopoli, a 16th-century masterpiece on the Grand Canal that stands as one of Venice's seven "magnificent palazzi." When George Clooney chose to marry Amal here in 2014, he wasn't just selecting a beautiful venue—he was confirming what travel cognoscenti already knew: this is the most extraordinary place to stay in Venice.

The palazzo retains its original Tiepolo frescoes, gilt leather walls, and Murano chandeliers while integrating Aman's subtle luxury approach. With just 24 suites across three floors, guests experience Venice as nobility did—arriving by private water taxi to their own landing stage, moving through rooms designed for entertaining, and gazing upon a canal that has reflected these walls for 500 years.

The Clooney Wedding

In September 2014, George Clooney and Amal Alamuddin's wedding transformed Aman Venice into the world's most famous venue. The civil ceremony took place in the palazzo's ornate ballroom beneath original 18th-century Tiepolo frescoes. The guest list—Matt Damon, Cindy Crawford, Bono, Anna Wintour—arrived by water taxi to a weekend of celebrations that captivated global media.

The Ceremony

Held in the palazzo's piano nobile (grand floor), with Tiepolo's frescoes overhead and the Grand Canal visible through tall windows. The setting required no decoration—500 years of Venetian history provided the backdrop.

The Weekend

Multiple events across Venice, but Aman served as home base. Guests stayed in suites, gathered in salons, and experienced Venice as Clooney's personal guests rather than tourists.

The Legacy

The wedding cemented Aman Venice's position as the ultimate Venice address. Inquiries for weddings and special celebrations surged. The palazzo became synonymous with celebrity-level events.

Palazzo Papadopoli

History

Built in the mid-16th century and expanded over centuries, the palazzo housed one of Venice's most powerful families. The Tiepolo frescoes were commissioned in the 18th century. Aman's restoration preserved every significant detail while adding modern comfort invisibly.

The Gardens

Extraordinarily for Venice, the palazzo includes two private gardens—a rarity that provides outdoor space impossible elsewhere. The garden suites offer direct access to this greenery, an almost unknown luxury in the floating city.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this where George Clooney got married?

Yes, George Clooney and Amal Alamuddin married at Aman Venice (Palazzo Papadopoli) in September 2014. The civil ceremony took place in the palazzo's piano nobile beneath original Tiepolo frescoes. The wedding made Aman Venice globally famous as the ultimate celebrity wedding venue.

What makes Aman Venice special?

Aman Venice occupies a 16th-century palazzo that is one of Venice's seven "magnificent palazzi." With just 24 suites, it offers an intimate experience in a building with museum-quality frescoes, private gardens (extraordinary for Venice), and a Grand Canal frontage. It combines Venetian historical grandeur with Aman's subtle luxury approach.

When is the best time to visit?

Venice is beautiful year-round. September (Film Festival) is busiest but most glamorous. November-February is quieter with atmospheric mist. April-June and September-October offer the best balance of weather and crowd levels. Carnival (February) and summer are peak tourist times.
