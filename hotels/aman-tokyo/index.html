Aman Tokyo

Urban Sanctuary of Minimalist Perfection

5-Star Aman Collection Otemachi, Tokyo
84 Rooms & Suites
¥150,000+ Per Night
2014 Established

Aman Tokyo represents the ultimate paradox: a sanctuary of absolute tranquility occupying the top floors of a skyscraper in one of the world's busiest cities. For celebrities seeking escape from Tokyo's intensity without leaving its heart, no other hotel comes close.

The first urban Aman property captures the brand's philosophy of peaceful retreat while incorporating traditional Japanese design elements—shoji screens, camphor wood, washi paper. Views stretch from the Imperial Palace gardens to Mount Fuji on clear days. The 2,500-square-meter spa is an experience unto itself, a journey through Japanese bathing culture elevated to art form.

The Aman Experience

"Aman" means peace in Sanskrit. Here, that peace is tangible.

Aman properties attract a specific type of traveler: those who value serenity over spectacle, space over amenities lists, and service that anticipates needs before they're expressed. The brand's celebrity following includes those seeking genuine escape rather than see-and-be-seen environments.

Minimalist Design

Every room features floor-to-ceiling windows, fusuma sliding doors, and a neutral palette of natural materials. Clutter doesn't exist. Space is the ultimate luxury.

Intuitive Service

Staff are trained to be present yet invisible. Needs are anticipated, preferences remembered, and privacy fiercely protected. No excessive formality—just quiet excellence.

Sense of Place

Despite its urban location, the hotel feels distinctly Japanese. Traditional materials, local artisan work, and cultural programming connect guests to authentic Japan.

Prime Tokyo Position

Otemachi Location

The hotel occupies the top six floors of the Otemachi Tower in Tokyo's financial district. Direct subway access connects to all of Tokyo, while the Imperial Palace gardens are steps away.

  • Imperial Palace: 5-minute walk
  • Ginza: 10-minute taxi
  • Tokyo Station: 8-minute walk
  • Roppongi: 15-minute taxi

Getting There

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do celebrities choose Aman Tokyo?

Aman Tokyo offers unparalleled privacy, serene design that provides genuine escape from public life, and service that anticipates needs without intrusion. The brand's discretion policy is legendary, and the minimalist aesthetic appeals to those who find typical luxury hotels overwhelming.

What makes Aman Tokyo different from other Tokyo luxury hotels?

While Tokyo has many excellent luxury hotels, Aman Tokyo offers significantly larger rooms (starting at 710 sq ft), a focus on Japanese design elements, and the Aman brand's signature peaceful atmosphere. The spa facilities are among the most extensive in the city.

Is Aman Tokyo worth the price?

For those who value space, design, tranquility, and exceptional spa facilities, Aman Tokyo justifies its premium pricing. The experience differs fundamentally from conventional luxury hotels—it's designed for those seeking genuine retreat rather than amenity checklists.

Can I use the spa without staying at the hotel?

The spa is primarily reserved for hotel guests. Day spa access may be available but is limited and should be arranged in advance. Staying at the hotel ensures full access to all facilities.
