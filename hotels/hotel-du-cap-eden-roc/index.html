Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc

The French Riviera's Most Legendary Address

5-Star Palace Cannes HQ Cap d'Antibes, France
117 Rooms & Suites
€1,500+ Per Night
1870 Established

Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc isn't merely a hotel—it's where the Cannes Film Festival actually lives. While the red carpet rolls in the Palais, the real drama unfolds on these legendary grounds, where Hollywood studios host their most exclusive parties and A-listers escape the festival frenzy.

Perched on 22 acres at the tip of Cap d'Antibes, the hotel has been the unofficial headquarters of cinema's elite since the 1920s. Fitzgerald wrote about it, Grace Kelly honeymooned here, and today's biggest stars continue the tradition. The Eden-Roc restaurant, carved into the rocks above the Mediterranean, may be the world's most glamorous outdoor dining experience.

The Cannes Film Festival Connection

For two weeks each May, this becomes Hollywood's Mediterranean headquarters.

During Cannes, the hotel transforms into an extension of the film industry itself. Every major studio books rooms and suites for their stars. The amFAR gala, cinema's most glamorous AIDS charity event, has been held here for decades. Getting a room during the festival requires connections and planning a year in advance—if not more.

Studio Takeovers

Warner Bros., Universal, and other studios book entire sections for their talent. Publicists set up war rooms, and the pool area becomes an unofficial deal-making floor.

The amFAR Gala

The annual AIDS benefit attracts the biggest names in entertainment. Held at the hotel since 1993, with auction items reaching millions and performances by global superstars.

Photo Calls

The hotel's iconic pool and Eden-Roc terrace serve as backdrops for countless magazine shoots and promotional photos during the festival.

22 Acres of Riviera Paradise

Eden-Roc Pavilion

The iconic waterfront pavilion houses the legendary restaurant, perched dramatically on the rocks. The terrace offers what many consider the world's most beautiful outdoor dining view. This is where deals are made, romances bloom, and legends are born.

The Seawater Pool

Cut directly into the rocks and fed by Mediterranean seawater, this pool is the most photographed in the world during Cannes. The surrounding sun deck is where you'll spot today's biggest stars.

Property Features

Private Beach

Rocky cove with floating platforms and pristine waters. Kayaks, paddleboards, and swimming in the crystal Mediterranean.

Parkland Gardens

22 acres of Mediterranean gardens, pine forests, and walking paths. Century-old trees and manicured lawns.

Tennis & Sports

Five clay tennis courts, fitness center, and jogging trails through the grounds. Historically hosted exhibition matches with tennis legends.

Spa Eden-Roc

La Mer spa treatments, indoor pool, and comprehensive wellness facilities. Facials and massages with sea views.

Private Cabanas

Bookable poolside and beachside cabanas for ultimate privacy. Many celebrities spend entire days in these exclusive spaces.

Helicopter Pad

Private helipad for arrivals from Nice, Monaco, or beyond. The preferred arrival method for many celebrities.

Booking Tips

Cannes Film Festival

Book 6-12 months ahead—or more. Many rooms are held by studios and production companies with long-standing relationships. Expect rates to triple or quadruple.

Summer Season

July-August is peak season with excellent weather. Book 3-4 months ahead for best suite selection. Minimum stays may apply.

Shoulder Seasons

April-early May and late September-October offer pleasant weather and better availability. Best value without the peak crowds.

Cash Policy

Historically famous for being cash-only, the hotel now accepts cards but maintains an old-world service philosophy that values discretion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc so famous?

The hotel has been the unofficial headquarters of the Cannes Film Festival since the 1920s. Its combination of stunning location, legendary service, and historic celebrity guest list makes it perhaps the world's most famous luxury hotel. F. Scott Fitzgerald immortalized it in "Tender Is the Night."

Can anyone stay at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc?

Yes, the hotel is open to all guests who can book and afford it. However, during Cannes Film Festival, rooms are extremely limited and largely pre-booked by studios and longtime clients. Outside festival season, availability is more accessible.

How far is the hotel from Cannes?

The hotel is located in Cap d'Antibes, approximately 20 minutes by car from the Cannes Film Festival venues. During the festival, the hotel provides shuttle services for guests attending screenings and events.

When is the best time to visit?

For celebrity watching, May during Cannes is unmatched. For a relaxing vacation with better availability and rates, consider late April, June, or September. Summer (July-August) offers peak weather but maximum crowds.
