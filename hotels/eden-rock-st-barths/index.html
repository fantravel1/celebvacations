Eden Rock - St Barths

The Caribbean's Most Iconic Address

5-Star Oetker Collection St. Jean Bay, St. Barthélemy
37 Rooms & Villas
$1,500+ Per Night
1995 Established

Eden Rock isn't just a hotel—it's the soul of St. Barths. Perched on a dramatic rocky promontory that divides St. Jean Bay into two perfect crescents of white sand, this Oetker Collection property has been the island's most celebrated address since David and Jane Matthews transformed a rocky outcrop into Caribbean legend.

The hotel survived Hurricane Irma in 2017 and emerged from a meticulous two-year restoration more beautiful than before. Every room, suite, and villa has been reimagined while preserving the bohemian-luxe spirit that originally attracted the global elite. The result is the Caribbean's most desirable hotel—intimate enough to feel exclusive, dramatic enough to take your breath away.

The Setting

The hotel's position is unmatched in the Caribbean. The rocky promontory rises from white sand beaches on both sides, providing 360-degree views of turquoise waters. Planes landing at the nearby Gustaf III Airport add to the drama—the approach over the hilltop is one of aviation's most thrilling, visible from the hotel's terraces.

St. Jean Bay (East)

The main beach with calm waters, excellent snorkeling, and the famous beach bar. Sun loungers on the sand, waiter service, and the best people-watching on the island.

St. Jean Bay (West)

More private stretch of beach with the hotel's beachfront suites and villas. Quieter, more exclusive, with the same stunning water.

The Eden Rock Story

David & Jane Matthews

In 1995, David Matthews—a British entrepreneur and former racing driver—purchased the rocky promontory on St. Jean Bay and began building what would become the Caribbean's most iconic hotel. His vision: a bohemian-luxe retreat with exceptional art and uncompromising standards.

The Royal Connection

When their son James Matthews married Pippa Middleton (sister of Catherine, Princess of Wales) in 2017, Eden Rock gained a royal adjacent connection. The hotel's society profile, already elevated, reached stratospheric heights.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Eden Rock St Barths so famous?

Eden Rock is famous for its dramatic position on a rocky promontory above St. Jean Bay, its celebrity guest list, and its connection to the British royal family (through Pippa Middleton's marriage to James Matthews, son of the founders). It's the Caribbean's most photographed hotel and the social epicenter of St. Barths.

When is the best time to visit Eden Rock?

December through April is peak season with perfect weather and the highest concentration of celebrities (especially New Year's). November and May offer excellent weather with lower rates. The hotel may close seasonally—check availability for summer and fall months.

How do you get to St. Barths?

St. Barths has no direct long-haul flights. Fly to St. Maarten (SXM) and take a short puddle-jumper flight (10 minutes) or ferry (45 minutes) to St. Barths. Private charter flights operate from multiple Caribbean islands. Eden Rock arranges all transfers.
