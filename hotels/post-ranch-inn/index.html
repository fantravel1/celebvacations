Post Ranch Inn

Where the Earth Meets the Sky

5-Star Adults Only Big Sur, California
40 Rooms
$1,200+ Per Night
1,200ft Above Pacific

Post Ranch Inn represents the pinnacle of environmental architecture and luxury hospitality. Perched on a cliff 1,200 feet above the Pacific Ocean, this adults-only retreat draws celebrities seeking genuine disconnection from the noise of fame. There are no TVs, no alarm clocks—just the endless ocean, redwood forests, and profound silence.

The property's eco-architecture—tree houses on stilts, sod-roofed buildings that disappear into the landscape, cliff-edge structures with walls of glass—predated the sustainability movement by decades. For guests, the result is the feeling of being suspended between wilderness and civilization, present in nature while cocooned in extraordinary comfort.

The Post Ranch Experience

Digital Detox

No TVs in rooms. No cell service (by design). WiFi available but discouraged. Post Ranch offers what celebrities rarely find: genuine freedom from connectivity. The silence is profound.

Privacy Absolute

Only 40 rooms on 100 acres. Adults only. Minimal common spaces. You may not see another guest for days. The architecture is designed to make each unit feel completely isolated.

Nature Immersion

Wake to fog rolling over cliffs, condors soaring past windows, whales breaching in the Pacific below. The location is genuinely dramatic—not staged, not manicured, simply wild California coast.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Post Ranch Inn famous?

Post Ranch Inn is famous for its extraordinary clifftop location 1,200 feet above the Pacific, its pioneering eco-architecture (tree houses, sod-roofed buildings), and its commitment to genuine digital disconnection. It's the ultimate California coast sanctuary and a renowned celebrity retreat.

Is Post Ranch Inn adults only?

Yes, Post Ranch Inn is strictly adults only (18+). This policy contributes to the profound tranquility and appeals to couples seeking romantic escape without children's activities or noise. It's a key part of the property's sanctuary atmosphere.

Is there cell service at Post Ranch Inn?

Cell service is extremely limited at Post Ranch Inn—by design. WiFi is available but the property encourages guests to disconnect. There are no TVs or alarm clocks in rooms. The digital detox is genuine and considered part of the experience.
