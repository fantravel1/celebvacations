One&Only Reethi Rah

The Maldives' Largest Private Island Resort

5-Star One&Only Resorts Private Island North Malé Atoll, Maldives
122 Villas
$2,500+ Per Night
44 Hectares
12 Beaches

One&Only Reethi Rah is not just another Maldives resort—it's a private country for celebrities seeking total escape. At 44 hectares, it's the largest private island in the Maldives, with 12 secluded beaches scattered along 6 kilometers of pristine shoreline. Where other resorts offer intimacy, Reethi Rah offers invisibility.

The resort's scale is its defining genius. With only 122 villas spread across this vast island, guests can spend their entire stay without encountering another soul. Tom Cruise chose Reethi Rah for exactly this reason—on an island this large, complete privacy isn't just promised, it's geometrically guaranteed. Each villa has its own stretch of beach, its own pool, its own piece of paradise. The staff-to-guest ratio approaches 6:1, ensuring anticipatory service without intrusion.

The Island

"Reethi Rah" means "beautiful island" in Dhivehi, the local language—an understatement of epic proportions. The island stretches in a curved shape across the North Malé Atoll, its irregular coastline creating countless private coves and beaches. The surrounding house reef offers world-class snorkeling and diving just steps from your villa, while the island's interior features manicured gardens, coconut palms, and banyan trees.

12 Secluded Beaches

Unlike resorts with a single beach where guests congregate, Reethi Rah's dozen beaches ensure you'll always find an empty stretch of sand. Some face sunrise, others sunset. Some are protected coves; others open to the ocean. Each villa has beach access, but exploring the island reveals hidden treasures.

The House Reef

The island is surrounded by pristine coral reef teeming with marine life. Step off the beach and you're in an aquarium—manta rays, reef sharks, sea turtles, and thousands of tropical fish. The resort's marine biology team leads conservation programs and dive experiences.

Private Lagoons

The overwater villas cluster around a protected lagoon on the island's western side, offering calm, crystalline water ideal for swimming and water sports. The water is shallow enough to wade, deep enough for water toys—and warm year-round.

The Discretion Policy

One&Only's staff are legendary for their discretion. Employees sign strict NDAs, and the resort operates a zero-tolerance policy for unauthorized photography. Arriving celebrities are met with rehearsed professionalism—staff are trained to treat all guests identically, whether they're honeymooners from Tokyo or Oscar winners from Hollywood. This culture of discretion explains why the resort's celebrity guest list is certainly longer than publicly documented.

Private Dining

For celebrities seeking even more seclusion, the resort offers countless private dining experiences: beachfront dinners under the stars, sandbank picnics accessible only by boat, overwater platform dinners, and of course, in-villa dining prepared by a private chef. The culinary team can recreate any restaurant menu in your villa—or create something entirely bespoke.

Experiences & Wellness

One&Only Spa

Set within a lush forest, the spa offers overwater treatment pavilions and a menu of Eastern and Western therapies. Visiting practitioners include experts in Ayurveda, Chinese medicine, and cutting-edge wellness. Multi-day programs address everything from stress recovery to fitness transformation.

Diving & Marine Center

PADI five-star dive center with access to some of the Maldives' best sites. Manta ray encounters, shark dives, and pristine coral gardens. The resort's marine biologist leads conservation programs including coral restoration projects guests can participate in.

Water Sports

Everything that floats: jet skis, parasailing, wakeboarding, kitesurfing, stand-up paddling, and kayaking. The resort's calm lagoon is ideal for learning, while the open ocean awaits adventurers. Private yacht charters for sunset cruises or island-hopping.

Fitness & Tennis

State-of-the-art gym with personal trainers, yoga pavilions for sunrise sessions, and two floodlit tennis courts with resident pro. Boxing, Pilates, and aqua fitness classes available. The resort has hosted professional tennis players for off-season training.

Kids & Teens

KidsOnly club offers supervised activities from marine biology lessons to arts and crafts. OneTribe teen program includes water sports, cooking classes, and DJ workshops. Celebrity families appreciate that children are entertained while parents enjoy adult time.

The One&Only Story

Island Transformation

The island was reimagined by the legendary Jean-Michel Gathy, the architect behind many of the world's finest resorts. His vision preserved the island's natural beauty while creating spaces of extraordinary sophistication. Every sight line, every pathway, every villa position was calculated for maximum beauty and privacy. The resort opened in 2005 and immediately redefined Maldives luxury.

One&Only Philosophy

Part of the prestigious One&Only Resorts portfolio (alongside properties in Dubai, Cape Town, and Mauritius), Reethi Rah embodies the brand's philosophy: genuine experiences in extraordinary locations with service that anticipates every need. The brand attracts guests who value privacy and understated excellence over flash and ostentation—explaining its strong appeal to celebrities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is One&Only Reethi Rah the top celebrity choice in the Maldives?

Size equals privacy. At 44 hectares with 12 secluded beaches, One&Only Reethi Rah is the largest private island resort in the Maldives—large enough that guests can spend their entire stay without encountering others. Combined with strict paparazzi policies, excellent security, world-class dining, and the One&Only brand's legendary service, it's the ultimate celebrity sanctuary.

How much does One&Only Reethi Rah cost per night?

Beach Villas start around $2,500 per night, while Water Villas with private pools begin at approximately $3,500 per night. The Grand Sunset Residence, the resort's most exclusive accommodation with four bedrooms and private beach, commands $25,000+ per night. Rates vary by season, with December-April being peak pricing.

How do you get to One&Only Reethi Rah?

The resort is located in the North Malé Atoll, a 45-minute luxury speedboat transfer from Velana International Airport (Malé). For a more dramatic arrival, seaplane transfers can be arranged offering aerial views of the atolls. Private yacht arrivals are also accommodated at the resort's jetty.

What celebrities have stayed at One&Only Reethi Rah?

One&Only Reethi Rah has hosted numerous A-list celebrities including Tom Cruise (multiple documented visits), David and Victoria Beckham with family, Lindsay Lohan for extended wellness retreats, Paul McCartney, Kate Moss, and various international royalty. The resort's strict privacy policies mean many celebrity visits go unreported.

When is the best time to visit One&Only Reethi Rah?

The Maldives' dry season runs from November to April, offering the best weather with calm seas and minimal rainfall. December through February is peak celebrity season, coinciding with New Year's and awards season breaks. The wet season (May-October) offers lower rates and fewer crowds, though brief tropical showers are common.

Is One&Only Reethi Rah family-friendly?

Yes, the resort welcomes families and offers excellent children's programs through the KidsOnly club. Multi-bedroom villas accommodate families comfortably, and the resort's size means children can play freely. Many celebrity families, including the Beckhams, have chosen Reethi Rah specifically for its family-friendly luxury.
