One&Only Reethi Rah

The Maldives' Largest Private Island Resort

5-Star One&Only Resorts North Malé Atoll, Maldives
122 Villas
$2,500+ Per Night
12 Beaches

One&Only Reethi Rah is the Maldives' most celebrated luxury resort, spreading across 44 hectares—making it the largest private island in the country. With 12 secluded beaches, celebrities can find complete privacy even during peak season.

The resort's size is its defining feature. Unlike smaller Maldivian properties where guests inevitably cross paths, Reethi Rah offers genuine seclusion. Tom Cruise has made it his Maldives base for this reason—on an island this large, privacy isn't just promised, it's guaranteed. The villas are spread across the island's perimeter, each with its own beach frontage and many with private pools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is One&Only Reethi Rah the top celebrity choice in the Maldives?

Size equals privacy. At 44 hectares with 12 beaches, the resort is large enough that guests can spend their entire stay without encountering others. Combined with strict paparazzi policies and excellent security, it's the ultimate celebrity sanctuary.

How do you get to One&Only Reethi Rah?

The resort is a 45-minute speedboat transfer from Malé International Airport, or celebrities can arrange seaplane transfers for more dramatic arrivals. Private yacht arrivals are also accommodated.
