The Little Nell

Aspen's Only Ski-In/Ski-Out Five-Star Hotel

5-Star Forbes Five-Star Aspen Mountain, Colorado
92 Rooms & Suites
$1,200+ Per Night
1989 Established

The Little Nell sits at the base of Aspen Mountain, directly adjacent to the Silver Queen Gondola. It's the only ski-in/ski-out five-star hotel in Aspen, making it the default choice for celebrities who want to maximize slope time while maintaining luxury standards.

The hotel combines mountain lodge warmth with sophisticated elegance. Belgian linens, gas fireplaces, and marble bathrooms create cocoons of comfort after days on the slopes. But it's the location and service that keep A-listers returning season after season—where else can you step out of your boots and into a five-star restaurant without crossing a parking lot?

Ski Concierge & Amenities

Ski Concierge

The Little Nell's legendary ski concierge service includes equipment storage, overnight boot warming, complimentary ski tuning, and personal assistance getting to the slopes. They'll even arrange private guides and heli-skiing adventures.

Element 47

The hotel's acclaimed restaurant offers contemporary American cuisine with an award-winning wine program (Element 47 refers to silver on the periodic table). A James Beard nominee and Aspen's finest dining.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do celebrities stay at The Little Nell?

The Little Nell offers unmatched convenience as Aspen's only ski-in/ski-out five-star hotel. Celebrities can ski directly to the hotel, avoiding shuttles and parking lots. The discrete service, exceptional dining, and central location make it the natural choice for high-profile guests.

When is peak celebrity season at The Little Nell?

Christmas through New Year's is Aspen's busiest celebrity period, with The Little Nell at the center of the action. Presidents' Day weekend and spring break also see significant A-list traffic.
