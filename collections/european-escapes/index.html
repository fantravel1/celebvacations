Celebrity European Escapes

From Italian lakes to Greek islands: The complete guide to where celebrities vacation across Europe.

Where Do Celebrities Vacation in Europe?

Quick Answer: Italy dominates with Lake Como (Clooney's home), Amalfi Coast, and Sardinia. The French Riviera (St. Tropez, Monaco, Cannes) attracts yacht culture. Greek islands Mykonos and Santorini bring summer parties. Ibiza remains the nightlife capital. London and Paris serve as year-round city escapes, especially during fashion weeks.

La Dolce Vita

Italy

Lake Como

George Clooney's Villa Oleandra put Como on the celebrity map. Mountain-ringed lake, historic villas, and refined Italian culture. Villa d'Este and Grand Hotel Tremezzo for stays.

Explore Lake Como →

Amalfi Coast

Positano, Ravello, and Capri draw celebrities for dramatic cliffs and Mediterranean beauty. Le Sirenuse, Belmond Hotel Caruso, and Il San Pietro. Honeymoon and anniversary favorite.

Capri

Island of the rich since Roman emperors. Piazzetta people-watching, Blue Grotto, and exclusive beach clubs. J.K. Rowling, Mariah Carey, and fashion crowd frequent.

Explore Capri →

Sardinia

Costa Smeralda's Porto Cervo is old money meets celebrity. Cala di Volpe hotel, exclusive marinas, yacht culture. European royalty and billionaires mix with stars.

Tuscany

Wine country retreats and historic estates. Sting's Il Palagio, Rosewood Castiglion del Bosco. Chianti vineyards, truffle hunting, cooking classes. Sophisticated celebrity escape.

Venice

Venice Film Festival in September draws global stars. Aman Venice, Hotel Cipriani, and Gritti Palace. George Clooney's wedding venue. Romantic but busy with tourists.

French Glamour

France

Paris

Fashion capital with year-round celebrity presence. Ritz Paris, Plaza Athénée, Le Bristol. Fashion weeks, film premieres, romantic getaways. Kim & Kanye wedding celebration venue.

Explore Paris →

St. Tropez

Beach club culture capital. Club 55, Nikki Beach, Pampelonne Beach. DiCaprio, supermodels, and yacht owners summer here. July-August peak season.

Monaco

Glamour and Grand Prix. Monte Carlo Casino, Hotel de Paris, superyacht harbor. Formula 1 weekend brings celebrities. Tax haven attracts resident celebrities.

Explore Monaco →

Cannes

Film Festival in May transforms this Riviera town. Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc, Martinez, Carlton. Year-round yacht culture. amfAR gala, Chopard parties.

Provence

Lavender fields and rosé culture. Brad Pitt's Château Miraval. Gordes, Luberon villages. Less scene than Riviera, more authenticity. Wine country charm.

Courchevel

Winter skiing for the ultra-wealthy. Russian billionaires, royalty, celebrities. Airelles and Les Trois Vallées luxury. Christmas and New Year peak.

Island Life

Greece & Mediterranean Islands

Mykonos

Summer party island with celebrity beach clubs. Nammos, Scorpios, SantAnna. Lindsay Lohan had a club here. Kardashians, models, and influencers flock July-August.

Explore Mykonos →

Santorini

Caldera views and romantic sunsets. Canaves Oia, Grace Hotel. More couples than party scene. Volcanic beaches, wine tasting. Instagram-famous destination.

Explore Santorini →

Ibiza

Nightlife capital of Europe. Ushuaïa, Pacha, DC10 draw celebrity DJs and partygoers. North for families, west for sunsets, clubs for nightlife. Leonardo DiCaprio regular.

Explore Ibiza →

Mallorca

Ibiza's quieter neighbor gaining celebrity attention. Belmond La Residencia in Deià. Rafael Nadal's home island. Family-friendly with beautiful coves.

Croatia

Dubrovnik (Game of Thrones fame), Hvar island parties. Yacht charter destination gaining celebrity momentum. Less crowded than Greece or France.

Montenegro

Rising celebrity destination. Porto Montenegro marina, Aman Sveti Stefan private island. Adriatic beauty without Riviera crowds. Watch this space.

British Isles

United Kingdom

London

Year-round celebrity hub. Claridge's, The Connaught, Chiltern Firehouse. Theatre, fashion, and business draw global stars. British celebrities' home base. Soho House culture.

Explore London →

English Countryside

Cotswolds cottages for David Beckham and Kate Moss. Soho Farmhouse members club. British celebrities escape London for country estates. Quiet luxury.

Common Questions

European Travel FAQs

Where do celebrities vacation in Europe?

Top celebrity European destinations include Lake Como (George Clooney's Italian home), the Amalfi Coast, French Riviera (St. Tropez, Monaco, Cannes), Greek islands (Mykonos, Santorini), Ibiza, London, and Paris.

When is the best time for celebrity European travel?

Summer (June-August) is peak for Mediterranean beaches and yachts. May brings Cannes Film Festival. September offers Venice Film Festival. February-March brings Paris Fashion Week. Winter sees celebrities in alpine resorts.
