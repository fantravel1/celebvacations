Frequently Asked Questions

How can I suggest a celebrity or destination to cover?

We welcome suggestions! Email us at hello@celebvacations.com with the celebrity or destination you'd like to see covered, along with any sources or evidence you're aware of.

I have information about a celebrity's travel. How do I share it?

We appreciate tips and corrections. Please email corrections@celebvacations.com with the relevant information and sources. We verify all information before publishing.

I represent a hotel or destination. How can we work together?

We're always interested in learning about properties and destinations that attract celebrity guests. Reach out to partners@celebvacations.com with details about your property and any documented celebrity visits.

Can I use CelebVacations content?

Our content is protected by copyright. For licensing inquiries or permission to republish, please contact hello@celebvacations.com.

How do I report a privacy concern?

We take privacy seriously. If you have concerns about any content on our site, please email corrections@celebvacations.com immediately and we will review your request.

About Our Response Times

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. For urgent corrections or privacy concerns, please indicate "URGENT" in your subject line.
