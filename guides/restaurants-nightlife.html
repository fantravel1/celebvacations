Restaurant & Nightlife FAQs

Celebrities eat at restaurants offering privacy, quality, and recognition. Global favorites include Nobu locations, Cipriani restaurants, and local institutions at each destination. They typically get the best tables (held for them or secured by concierge), use private dining rooms, and visit during off-peak hours. Many restaurants have unofficial celebrity policies ensuring discretion.

Yes! Most celebrity restaurants are open to all - they're just hard to book. Tips: Book 30+ days ahead, try lunch instead of dinner, consider bar seating (same food, easier access), use concierge services if staying at affiliated hotels, or try walking in for cancellations. Apps like Resy can help secure reservations at popular spots.

Ibiza celebrity spots include: Blue Marlin beach club, Nikki Beach, Nobu Hotel Ibiza Bay, Lio cabaret, Pacha nightclub (VIP areas), Destino Pacha Resort, and private villa parties. During the day, celebrities frequent beach clubs or yacht at Formentera. Evenings move to restaurants like Cipriani and clubs. Many stay in private villas in Es Cubells or near Cala Jondal.
