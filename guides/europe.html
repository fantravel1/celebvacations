Celebrity Destinations in Italy

#1 Italy

Europe FAQs

Top European celebrity destinations: Lake Como (Italy), Ibiza (Spain), Mykonos (Greece), Amalfi Coast (Italy), Saint-Tropez (France), London, Paris, Monaco, Capri (Italy), Sardinia (Italy), Santorini (Greece), and ski resorts in Switzerland and France. Summer is Mediterranean dominated, while cities are year-round.

Peak celebrity Europe travel: Summer (June-September) for Mediterranean islands and yacht cruises. Cannes Film Festival (May), Venice Film Festival (September), Paris Fashion Weeks (March, October), and ski season (December-March) for Alps. Monaco Grand Prix (May) brings racing and entertainment celebrities.
