Celebrity Beach Destinations

#1 West Coast

Malibu, California

27 miles of celebrity coastline. Jennifer Aniston, Leonardo DiCaprio, Lady Gaga own beachfront. Nobu Malibu is the restaurant. Point Dume for privacy.

342 celebrity visitsYear-round
#1 East Coast

The Hamptons, New York

East Coast summer capital. Gwyneth Paltrow, Jerry Seinfeld estates. Farm-to-table dining, exclusive beach clubs, celebrity charity events.

198 celebrity visitsPeak: Jun-Aug

Miami, Florida

Year-round celebrity playground. Art Basel, South Beach, Wynwood. David Beckham, DJ Khaled home base. Carbone Miami is the restaurant.

289 celebrity visitsYear-round

Hawaii (Maui & Kauai)

Island escape for celebrities. Maui's Wailea (Four Seasons, Montage), Kauai's North Shore for privacy. Oprah, Jennifer Aniston own estates.

Celebrity Ski & Mountain Destinations

America's premier celebrity ski destination. Mariah Carey, Hadid sisters, Hollywood elite. The Little Nell, Cloud Nine apres-ski. Four mountains.

US FAQs

Top US celebrity destinations: Malibu (beach lifestyle), Hamptons (East Coast summer), Aspen (ski capital), Miami (year-round warm weather), Hawaii (island escape), New York City and Los Angeles (home bases), Napa Valley (wine country), and Jackson Hole (mountain privacy). Many own homes in Malibu and Hamptons.

California celebrity spots: Malibu (beach homes), Beverly Hills and Bel-Air (mansions), Hollywood Hills (views), Malibu (Nobu, beach clubs), Napa Valley (wine weekends), Big Sur (romantic escapes), Lake Tahoe (summer and ski), and Palm Springs (desert retreats, Coachella time).
