Caribbean FAQs

Top Caribbean celebrity destinations: St. Barts (#1, especially Dec-Jan holidays), Turks & Caicos (Grace Bay, Amanyara), Barbados (Sandy Lane, west coast), Mustique (ultra-exclusive villas), Anguilla (Four Seasons, quiet luxury), Bahamas (private islands in Exumas), Jamaica (GoldenEye), and BVI for yacht charters.

St. Barts offers: French Caribbean elegance and cuisine, celebrity-friendly privacy culture, world-class hotels (Eden Rock, Le Toiny), legendary New Year's parties, no mass tourism or cruise ships, beautiful beaches, and a social calendar that attracts peers. It's become tradition for fashion and entertainment elite.

Peak celebrity season: December through April (dry season, holiday escapes). Christmas/New Year's sees highest concentration in St. Barts. February-March good for post-awards season trips. Summer is hurricane season and fewer celebrities, but lower prices. Shoulder seasons (Nov, May) offer balance of weather and availability.
