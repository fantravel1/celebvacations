The world's most exclusive islands and beaches where celebrities escape for sun, sand, and privacy.

Caribbean

Celebrity Caribbean Islands

The Caribbean is celebrity vacation headquarters, especially December through April.

#1 Caribbean

St. Barts

The undisputed champion. French Caribbean elegance, celebrity-friendly culture, New Year's Eve legends. Leonardo DiCaprio, Beyonce, and fashion elite make this home base for winter holidays.

218 celebrity visitsPeak: Dec-Jan
#2 Caribbean

Turks & Caicos

Kardashian-Jenner family favorite. Grace Bay Beach consistently ranked world's best. Amanyara resort is celebrity central. More family-friendly than St. Barts' party scene.

98 celebrity visitsPeak: Dec-Apr

Barbados

Rihanna's homeland draws her fans and fellow celebrities. Sandy Lane resort hosted Tiger Woods' wedding. British celebrities especially love it. More local culture than other Caribbean islands.

76 celebrity visitsPeak: Dec-Apr

Mustique

The most exclusive. No hotels - villa rentals only. Princess Margaret, Mick Jagger, Tommy Hilfiger own properties. Kate Middleton's family holidays here. Paparazzi forbidden, privacy guaranteed.

Private accessBy invitation
Mediterranean

Celebrity Mediterranean Islands

Summer headquarters for A-list beach escapes.

#1 Mediterranean

Ibiza, Spain

Party capital with sophisticated beach club scene. Musicians, DJs, supermodels congregate. Blue Marlin, Nikki Beach, and private villas in Es Cubells.

Mykonos, Greece

Greek island glamour. Nammos beach club is celebrity central. Yacht culture, whitewashed streets, and Mediterranean perfection.

Capri, Italy

Italian island chic. Blue Grotto, designer shopping, Fontelina beach club. Mariah Carey, Naomi Campbell, fashion industry base.
Exotic Escapes

Indian Ocean & Pacific Islands

Maldives

Private island resorts with overwater villas. Ultimate honeymoon destination. Complete isolation from the world. Soneva, One&Only, Cheval Blanc are celebrity favorites.

142 celebrity visitsPeak: Nov-Apr

Bali, Indonesia

Wellness and spirituality meet tropical beauty. Ubud for retreats, Seminyak for beach clubs. Chrissy Teigen, John Legend, and wellness-focused celebrities.

112 celebrity visitsPeak: Apr-Oct

Bora Bora, French Polynesia

Overwater bungalows with Mount Otemanu views. Four Seasons and Conrad are celebrity choices. Honeymoon paradise, bucket list destination.

87 celebrity visitsPeak: May-Oct

Fiji

Remote private islands. Laucala Island, Wakaya Club, COMO Laucala. Ultimate privacy for those seeking complete isolation. Long journey rewards with paradise.

45 celebrity visitsYear-round

Island & Beach FAQs

Top celebrity islands by region: Caribbean (St. Barts, Turks & Caicos, Barbados, Mustique, Anguilla), Mediterranean (Ibiza, Mykonos, Capri, Sardinia), Indian Ocean (Maldives, Seychelles), Pacific (Bora Bora, Fiji, Hawaii), and Indonesia (Bali). St. Barts and Maldives lead overall for celebrity frequency.

Caribbean celebrity hotspots: (1) St. Barts - the #1 choice, especially December-January, (2) Turks & Caicos - Kardashian favorite, family-friendly, (3) Barbados - British celebrities, Rihanna connection, (4) Mustique - ultra-exclusive villa rentals, (5) Anguilla - quiet luxury, (6) Bahamas - private islands like Exumas, (7) St. Lucia - honeymoons, (8) BVI - yacht charters.

Celebrity Hawaii destinations: Maui (Wailea for resorts like Four Seasons and Montage, Hana for privacy), Kauai (North Shore estates, St. Regis Princeville), Big Island (Hualalai resort, private estates). Many celebrities own or rent private properties rather than staying at hotels. Jennifer Aniston, Oprah, and tech billionaires have Maui estates.

Top tropical celebrity vacations: (1) Maldives private island resorts for ultimate privacy, (2) St. Barts for the scene and social calendar, (3) Bora Bora for honeymoons and special occasions, (4) Turks & Caicos for family beach trips, (5) Bali for wellness retreats, (6) Fiji for remote island escapes, (7) Seychelles for Indian Ocean alternative to Maldives.
