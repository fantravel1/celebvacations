Ski slopes and tropical escapes. Two paths to celebrity winter bliss.

December - March

Celebrity Ski Destinations

Where A-listers hit the slopes and enjoy apres-ski.

#1 Ski Destination

Aspen, Colorado

America's premier celebrity ski destination. Mariah Carey, the Hadid sisters, and Hollywood's elite hit the slopes. Four mountains, world-class dining, and apres-ski at Cloud Nine. The Little Nell and St. Regis are celebrity hotels of choice.

186 winter visits Peak: Dec-Mar
#2 Ski

St. Moritz, Switzerland

The original winter resort destination. European royalty, Formula 1 drivers, and old-money celebrities. Badrutt's Palace Hotel is legendary. Polo on frozen lake, Cresta Run bobsled, and ultimate Alpine luxury.

94 winter visits Peak: Dec-Feb

Courchevel, France

French Alps luxury at its peak. Russian oligarchs and international celebrities. The famous altiport for private planes, Le Chabichou restaurant, and ultra-exclusive Courchevel 1850 village.

78 winter visits Peak: Dec-Feb

Whistler, British Columbia

North America's largest ski resort. Justin Bieber, Ryan Reynolds (Vancouver native), and celebrities seeking world-class terrain. Less paparazzi than Aspen, epic skiing, and vibrant village scene.

67 winter visits Peak: Dec-Mar
Escaping the Cold

Winter Tropical Destinations

Trading snow boots for flip flops at these celebrity-favorite warm weather escapes.

#1 Winter Beach

St. Barts, Caribbean

THE winter celebrity destination. Christmas through New Year's sees the highest concentration of A-listers anywhere. Leonardo DiCaprio, Beyonce, P. Diddy, and the fashion elite. Eden Rock and Hotel Le Toiny are celebrity hotels. New Year's Eve on the island is legendary.

218 winter visits Peak: Dec-Jan
#2 Winter Beach

Maldives

Ultimate privacy in overwater villas. Winter is prime season (dry weather). Honeymoons and romantic escapes dominate. Soneva, One&Only, and Cheval Blanc are top celebrity resorts. Complete isolation from the world.

142 winter visits Peak: Nov-Apr

Turks & Caicos

The Kardashian-Jenner family's favorite. Providenciales (Provo) has Grace Bay Beach, consistently rated world's best. Amanyara resort is celebrity central. Less party scene than St. Barts, more family-friendly luxury.

98 winter visits Peak: Dec-Apr

Barbados

Rihanna's homeland is a celebrity favorite. Sandy Lane resort hosted Tiger Woods' wedding. West Coast beaches, rum culture, and a more laid-back Caribbean vibe. Simon Cowell, Lewis Hamilton, and British celebrities love it here.

76 winter visits Peak: Dec-Apr

Celebrity Christmas & New Year's

The holiday period is peak celebrity travel season.

Where Celebrities Spend the Holidays

Christmas Week

Many celebrities spend Christmas with family before traveling. Aspen is popular for Christmas ski trips, while some fly to St. Barts or the Caribbean for a beach Christmas. Private jets to warm destinations spike on December 23-24.

New Year's Eve

St. Barts is the #1 celebrity New Year's destination. Parties at Nikki Beach and Eden Rock are legendary. Miami hosts major celebrity events, and Dubai has become a rising destination. Aspen remains strong for those preferring mountains.

Post-Awards Recovery (February-March)

After the grueling awards season circuit (Golden Globes, Grammys, Oscars), celebrities often escape. Tropical destinations like the Maldives, Turks & Caicos, and wellness retreats in Costa Rica see spikes in celebrity bookings.

Winter Vacation FAQs

Celebrities vacation in two main categories during winter: ski destinations (Aspen, St. Moritz, Courchevel, Whistler, Deer Valley) and tropical escapes (St. Barts, Maldives, Turks & Caicos, Barbados, Bahamas). St. Barts during December/January is the single highest concentration of celebrities at any destination during the year, while Aspen dominates for ski holidays.

Top celebrity ski destinations: (1) Aspen, Colorado - America's #1 celebrity ski spot with Mariah Carey, the Hadids, and Hollywood elite, (2) St. Moritz, Switzerland - European royalty and billionaires, (3) Courchevel, France - French Alps ultra-luxury, (4) Whistler, Canada - world-class terrain, (5) Deer Valley, Utah - exclusive ski-in/ski-out, (6) Jackson Hole, Wyoming - for more adventurous celebrities.

St. Barts dominates celebrity holiday travel. New Year's Eve parties at Eden Rock and Nikki Beach are legendary. Other popular spots: Aspen for ski holidays, Maldives for complete isolation, Miami for NYE parties, and Dubai as a rising destination. The Kardashians often do Aspen for Christmas, while music industry celebrities tend toward St. Barts and Miami.

Top celebrity hotels in Aspen: (1) The Little Nell - ski-in/ski-out at Aspen Mountain base, the ultimate celebrity hotel, (2) St. Regis Aspen - luxury with great location, (3) The Limelight Hotel - modern and convenient, (4) Private rental homes - many celebrities rent large chalets. Celebrity restaurants include Matsuhisa, Cache Cache, and the apres-ski scene at Cloud Nine Alpine Bistro.

For celebrity sightings, December 20 - January 5 is peak season in St. Barts (but most expensive). February-March offers better rates with still-good weather. Dry season runs December-April. Hurricane season (June-November) is off-season but has significant discounts. For the best balance of celebrity activity and value, late January or February is ideal.
