Travel Profile

Megan Fox's travel balances tropical beach vacations with her three sons (Noah, Bodhi, Journey from her marriage to Brian Austin Green) and film work that takes her to various locations.

Puerto Rico has been documented multiple times. Hawaii provides family beach time. Costa Rica represents the wellness retreat aesthetic she's embraced. Her spirituality and interest in alternative wellness influences destination choices.

Born in Tennessee and raised in Florida, she moved to Los Angeles for her acting career. She rose to fame with Transformers and has maintained a steady film career alongside raising her children.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Megan Fox live?

Megan Fox lives in the Los Angeles area, reportedly in the Calabasas area. She raises her three sons Noah, Bodhi, and Journey there. She previously shared a Malibu home with Brian Austin Green before their divorce.

Where is Megan Fox from?

Megan Fox was born in Oak Ridge, Tennessee and raised in Rockwood, Tennessee before moving to Port St. Lucie, Florida. She began acting and modeling as a teenager before moving to Los Angeles to pursue her career full-time.
