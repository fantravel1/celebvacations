Travel Profile

Gordon Ramsay's travel is dominated by his global restaurant empire and TV productions, but Cornwall has become the family's beloved escape. His famous triathlon training means active vacations are common.

Cornwall, England is the family retreat—their £4 million estate became famous during COVID lockdowns. The Maldives represents luxury beach escape. His restaurants span London, Las Vegas, Dubai, and beyond, making business travel constant.

Born in Scotland and raised partly in England, he trained in Paris and worked at top restaurants before building his empire. Wife Tana and their six children (Megan, twins Holly and Jack, Matilda, Oscar, and Jesse) feature in his documented travels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Gordon Ramsay live?

Gordon Ramsay splits time between London (his primary residence), Los Angeles (for TV work), and Cornwall (family beach estate). His London home in Wandsworth and Cornwall estate are the main UK properties.

Where is Gordon Ramsay from?

Gordon Ramsay was born in Johnstone, Scotland and moved to Stratford-upon-Avon, England as a child. He trained as a chef in London and Paris before opening his first restaurant. He identifies primarily as British.
