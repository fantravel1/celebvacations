Travel Profile

Bad Bunny (Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio) maintains deep ties to his Puerto Rican roots even as one of the world's biggest music stars. His vacation style reflects Caribbean culture—beaches, boats, and family time.

Puerto Rico remains his primary base, with property in his hometown area of Vega Baja and in San Juan. The Dominican Republic is a frequent destination, reflecting the close cultural ties between the islands and collaborations with Dominican artists.

His rise to global fame has expanded his travel—European fashion weeks, Italian vacations, and Los Angeles for work and leisure. He's also passionate about WWE and has appeared at wrestling events in various cities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Bad Bunny from?

Bad Bunny (Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio) is from Vega Baja, Puerto Rico. He maintains strong ties to the island and is known for championing Puerto Rican culture and causes in his music and public appearances.

Where does Bad Bunny live?

Bad Bunny maintains properties in Puerto Rico, including in his hometown area. He also has a presence in Los Angeles for his music and acting career. He frequently travels but stays connected to his Puerto Rican roots.
