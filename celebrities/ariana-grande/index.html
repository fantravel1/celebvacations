Travel Profile

Ariana Grande's travel has evolved from tour-centric globetrotting to more balanced work-leisure trips. Her role as Glinda in the Wicked films brought extended stays in London.

Lake Como, Italy has emerged as a favorite retreat, aligning with her appreciation for Italian culture (her family is of Italian descent). Hawaii provides tropical escapes when she's stateside.

Born in Boca Raton, Florida, she maintains Los Angeles as her primary US base. The UK has become a second home due to Wicked filming at London's studios, and she's developed an appreciation for British culture during her extended stays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Ariana Grande from?

Ariana Grande was born and raised in Boca Raton, Florida. She began her career on Broadway before moving to Los Angeles for her role on Nickelodeon's Victorious. She's of Italian descent on both sides of her family.

Where did Ariana Grande film Wicked?

The Wicked films were filmed at studios in England, requiring Ariana Grande to spend extended time in the UK. This has included stays in and around London while filming her role as Glinda.
