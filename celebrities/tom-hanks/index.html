Travel Profile

Tom Hanks and wife Rita Wilson have Greek citizenship—granted in 2020 for their support of Greece after wildfires. Rita's Greek heritage makes Greece their special vacation destination, with property on the island of Antiparos.

They've owned a house in Antiparos since the late 1980s and summer there regularly. Australia was significant—they were there in 2020 filming the Elvis biopic when they contracted COVID-19, becoming early celebrity cases.

Born in Concord, California, Tom has become one of America's most beloved actors. His 35+ year marriage to Rita Wilson is rare in Hollywood, and they frequently travel together for both work and leisure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Tom Hanks live?

Tom Hanks lives primarily in Pacific Palisades, Los Angeles with wife Rita Wilson. They also own property in Antiparos, Greece, where they spend summers. In 2020, they were granted Greek citizenship for their cultural contributions to Greece.

Where is Tom Hanks from?

Tom Hanks was born in Concord, California and grew up moving between California and elsewhere with his father after his parents divorced. He studied theater in California before moving to New York to pursue acting professionally.
