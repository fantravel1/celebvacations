Travel Profile

Zac Efron has evolved from teen star to travel documentary host, with his Netflix series "Down to Earth" showcasing his genuine interest in sustainable living and global cultures.

Australia—particularly Byron Bay—became a significant destination when he spent extended time there during 2020-2021. He's spoken about the appeal of the laid-back Australian lifestyle compared to Hollywood.

His travel interests lean toward wellness, sustainability, and adventure. Costa Rica's eco-tourism, Iceland's landscapes, and Bali's wellness culture have all featured in his documented travels. He often travels with his brother Dylan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Did Zac Efron move to Australia?

Zac Efron spent extended time in Australia, particularly Byron Bay, during 2020-2021 and has spoken highly of the Australian lifestyle. While he maintains his LA base, Australia became a significant second home during that period.

What is Zac Efron's travel show?

"Down to Earth with Zac Efron" is his Netflix travel series where he explores sustainable living practices around the world. The show visits locations including Iceland, Costa Rica, Sardinia, and more.
