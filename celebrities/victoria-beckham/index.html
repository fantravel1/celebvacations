Travel Profile

Victoria Beckham's travel balances her fashion business (shows in London, Paris, New York, Milan) with family vacations alongside husband David and their four children: Brooklyn, Romeo, Cruz, and Harper.

The Cotswolds estate is the family's English country retreat—a contrast to their London city life. Mediterranean summers involve yacht trips along the Croatian and Italian coasts. The Maldives provides tropical luxury.

As Posh Spice in the Spice Girls, she toured globally. Now her fashion brand takes her to international fashion weeks, while David's Inter Miami ownership means regular Miami visits as a family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Victoria Beckham live?

Victoria Beckham lives primarily in London, with a country estate in the Cotswolds and a Miami penthouse. The London base is central to her Victoria Beckham fashion brand headquarters. The family splits time between all three properties.

Where is Victoria Beckham from?

Victoria Beckham (née Adams) was born in Harlow, Essex, England. She grew up in Hertfordshire in an affluent family. She rose to fame as "Posh Spice" in the Spice Girls before transitioning to fashion design.
