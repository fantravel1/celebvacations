Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Jason Momoa live?

Jason Momoa lives in the Topanga Canyon area of Los Angeles, California. He previously lived with Lisa Bonet before their 2022 separation. He maintains strong ties to Hawaii and travels there frequently, calling it his true home.

Where is Jason Momoa from?

Jason Momoa was born in Honolulu, Hawaii on August 1, 1979. His father is of Hawaiian, German, Irish, and Native American descent, while his mother is of German, Irish, and Native American descent. He was raised mostly on Oahu and identifies strongly with Hawaiian culture.
