Travel Profile

Ciara and husband Russell Wilson document their family travels extensively on social media. Their vacations typically align with Russell's NFL off-season, allowing for extended tropical getaways.

Mexico (Cabo) is a documented favorite. The Maldives represents ultimate luxury escape. Hawaii provides family beach time. Their blended family of four children (Future, Sienna, Win, Amora) travels together on vacation.

Born Ciara Princess Harris in Austin, Texas, she moved frequently as a child due to her father's military career. She rose to fame with "Goodies" in 2004 and has sold millions of records. Her marriage to Russell Wilson in 2016 created one of entertainment's power couples.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Ciara live?

Ciara's residence moves with her husband Russell Wilson's NFL team. They've had homes in Seattle and Denver, while maintaining property in Los Angeles for her music career. They own multiple properties.

Where is Ciara from?

Ciara was born in Austin, Texas but moved frequently as a child because her father was in the US Army. She's lived in various states including New York, Arizona, and Georgia (Atlanta) where she signed her first record deal.
