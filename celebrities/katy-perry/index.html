Travel Profile

Katy Perry's travel has evolved from pop star touring to family vacations with fiancé Orlando Bloom and their daughter Daisy Dove. The couple shares a love for adventure and beach destinations.

Hawaii is a regular escape—Orlando is an avid paddleboarder and surfer, and they've been photographed frequently on Hawaiian beaches. Italy and Greece feature in their European travel, with Mediterranean yacht trips documented.

Her Las Vegas residency at Resorts World kept her anchored there from 2021-2023. Born in Santa Barbara, California, she maintains LA as her primary base while the family splits time with Orlando's UK connections.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom married?

Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom have been engaged since 2019 but have not publicly married as of 2025. They have a daughter together, Daisy Dove Bloom, born in August 2020. Orlando also has a son, Flynn, from his previous marriage to Miranda Kerr.

Where does Katy Perry live?

Katy Perry lives in Los Angeles with Orlando Bloom and their daughter Daisy. She's owned various properties in the LA area, including a famous dispute over a former convent in Los Feliz. The family also has connections to the UK.
