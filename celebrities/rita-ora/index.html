Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Rita Ora live?

Rita Ora maintains homes in London (her primary base) and Los Angeles. She married director Taika Waititi in 2022. Her jet-set lifestyle means she's frequently traveling between continents for music, TV, and fashion work.

Where is Rita Ora from?

Rita Ora was born in Pristina, Kosovo (then part of Yugoslavia) to Albanian parents. Her family fled as refugees to London when she was a baby due to the Kosovo conflict. She grew up in West London and considers herself British-Albanian.
