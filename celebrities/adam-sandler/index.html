Travel Profile

Adam Sandler famously combines work and vacation—his Happy Madison productions often film in paradise locations like Hawaii. His casual style (seen in viral photos wearing basketball shorts everywhere) extends to laid-back vacation choices.

Hawaii is practically a second home—multiple films have shot there (Just Go with It, 50 First Dates). His daughters Sadie and Sunny often appear in his films, making film shoots family trips. Basketball pick-up games are a travel constant.

Born in Brooklyn, New York and raised in New Hampshire, he rose to fame on SNL before becoming a comedy film mogul. His Malibu home provides beach lifestyle year-round.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Adam Sandler live?

Adam Sandler lives in Malibu, California with his wife Jackie and daughters Sadie and Sunny. The Malibu beach lifestyle fits his casual personality. He also has property in Los Angeles and spends significant time in New York.

Where is Adam Sandler from?

Adam Sandler was born in Brooklyn, New York but raised in Manchester, New Hampshire. He went to NYU and started in comedy clubs in New York before joining SNL in 1990. His films often feature New York settings.
