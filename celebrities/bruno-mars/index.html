Travel Profile

Bruno Mars (Peter Gene Hernandez) grew up in Waikiki, Hawaii, and maintains deep connections to the islands. His multicultural background—Puerto Rican, Filipino, with Ashkenazi Jewish heritage—reflects in his eclectic musical style.

His Las Vegas residency keeps him anchored to that city, where he's performed for years. His Silk Sonic collaboration with Anderson .Paak brought Grammy glory and additional touring. Hawaii remains his retreat from the spotlight.

He maintains a relatively private personal life compared to other artists of his caliber, preferring low-key family time over heavily documented celebrity vacations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Bruno Mars from?

Bruno Mars was born Peter Gene Hernandez in Honolulu, Hawaii. He grew up in the Waikiki neighborhood and performed in his family's band from a young age. He's of Puerto Rican and Filipino descent with some Ashkenazi Jewish heritage.

Where does Bruno Mars perform in Las Vegas?

Bruno Mars has had a long-running Las Vegas residency at Park MGM. His Silk Sonic shows with Anderson .Paak also took place in Vegas. The residency keeps him based in Las Vegas for extended periods.
