Travel Profile

Miley Cyrus (born Destiny Hope Cyrus) maintains a strong connection to Malibu, where she rebuilt after losing her home in the 2018 Woolsey Fire. The beachside city has been central to her life for years.

Born in Nashville, Tennessee to country music star Billy Ray Cyrus, she maintains connections to the country music scene and visits family there. Her animal rescue work influences her lifestyle, and she often stays close to home with her many pets.

Post-"Flowers" Grammy success, she's maintained a relatively low profile for vacations, preferring private time over highly documented celebrity trips.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Miley Cyrus live?

Miley Cyrus lives in Malibu, California. She lost her previous Malibu home in the devastating 2018 Woolsey Fire and has since rebuilt. Malibu's beach lifestyle aligns with her personality.

Where is Miley Cyrus from?

Miley Cyrus was born in Nashville, Tennessee. Her father is country music star Billy Ray Cyrus (known for "Achy Breaky Heart"). She was named Destiny Hope Cyrus at birth; "Miley" evolved from her childhood nickname "Smiley."
