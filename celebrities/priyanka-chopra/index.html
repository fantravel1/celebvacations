Travel Profile

Priyanka Chopra Jonas lives a truly global life—working in both Bollywood and Hollywood means constant travel between India, the US, and UK. Her marriage to Nick Jonas adds the Jonas Brothers' tour schedule to the mix.

India remains central—she visits Mumbai and family regularly, and has business interests there including a restaurant. The Maldives is a documented favorite for tropical escapes. Their elaborate Indian wedding in Jodhpur showcased her connection to Indian heritage.

Born in Jamshedpur, India, she won Miss World 2000 before becoming one of Bollywood's highest-paid actresses. Her transition to Hollywood (Quantico, Citadel) expanded her global presence significantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Priyanka Chopra live?

Priyanka Chopra Jonas lives primarily in Los Angeles (Encino area) with husband Nick Jonas and daughter Malti Marie. She also has property in London and maintains a home in Mumbai, India. Her truly global lifestyle means she's frequently traveling between all three.

Where is Priyanka Chopra from?

Priyanka Chopra was born in Jamshedpur, India. Her parents were both doctors in the Indian Army, so she moved frequently as a child. She studied partly in the US before returning to India and winning Miss World 2000.
