Travel Profile

Russell Wilson and wife Ciara (R&B singer) are one of the NFL's power couples. Their vacations are documented on social media and typically feature tropical beach destinations during the NFL off-season.

Mexico (Cabo San Lucas) is a frequent destination. Hawaii provides island escapes. Caribbean destinations offer additional beach options. Their blended family of four children (Future, Sienna, Win, Amora) travels together.

Born in Cincinnati, Ohio and raised partly in Virginia, he played college football at Wisconsin before being drafted by the Seattle Seahawks (Super Bowl XLVIII champion). He's now playing elsewhere after a trade to the Denver Broncos and subsequent moves.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Russell Wilson live?

Russell Wilson's residence depends on his current NFL team. He and wife Ciara have had homes in Seattle (during his Seahawks years), Denver, and maintain property in Los Angeles for Ciara's music career.

Where is Russell Wilson from?

Russell Wilson was born in Cincinnati, Ohio and moved to Richmond, Virginia as a child, where he attended Collegiate School. He played college football at NC State and Wisconsin before being drafted by the Seattle Seahawks in 2012.
