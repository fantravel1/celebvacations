Travel Profile

Florence Pugh, the Oscar-nominated British actress known for "Little Women," "Midsommar," and her role as Yelena Belova in the Marvel universe, brings her signature boldness to her travel adventures. She's refreshingly open about her experiences on social media.

Known for her love of cooking and food, Florence often documents culinary experiences during her travels. She balances glamorous Mediterranean summers with cozy English countryside retreats, showing the same authenticity in her vacation style as she does in her acting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Florence Pugh vacation?

Florence Pugh enjoys vacations in Ibiza, Italy, the English countryside, and various Mediterranean destinations. She's known for sharing her travels on social media and enjoying both glamorous getaways and quiet countryside retreats.

Where is Florence Pugh from?

Florence Pugh is from Oxford, England. She maintains strong ties to the UK and often returns to the English countryside for breaks from her busy Hollywood schedule.

What type of vacations does Florence Pugh prefer?

Florence Pugh enjoys a mix of party destinations like Ibiza, romantic European getaways in Italy, and peaceful retreats in the English countryside. She's also an avid cook and often shares food experiences from her travels.
