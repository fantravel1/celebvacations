Travel Profile

Austin Butler's breakout role as Elvis Presley required him to spend nearly three years in Australia, profoundly shaping his travel perspective. The experience gave him a deep appreciation for immersive travel and exploring new cultures.

Since his Oscar-nominated performance, Austin has been in high demand, with extensive travel for film festivals, premieres, and new projects. He balances high-profile events with more low-key getaways when his schedule permits.
