Travel Profile

Neymar Jr is known for his vibrant lifestyle both on and off the pitch. His vacations often make headlines—from Ibiza yacht parties to legendary birthday celebrations in Brazil.

Brazil remains central to his identity, with frequent returns to Rio de Janeiro and his hometown area. His mansion in Mangaratiba (near Rio) is a favorite retreat. Ibiza is the European summer go-to, often involving celebrity-filled yacht parties.

His 2023 move to Al-Hilal in Saudi Arabia brought a new base, though he maintains properties and lifestyle connections in Europe and Brazil. The Maldives and various tropical destinations feature in his vacation repertoire.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Neymar live now?

Neymar currently plays for Al-Hilal in Saudi Arabia after his 2023 transfer from PSG. He maintains properties in Brazil, including a mansion in Mangaratiba near Rio de Janeiro, and connections to Europe from his Barcelona and Paris years.

Where does Neymar go on vacation?

Neymar's favorite vacation destinations include Ibiza (for summer parties), Brazil (homeland visits), the Maldives, and various Mediterranean yacht destinations. His birthday parties in Brazil are legendary for their scale and guest lists.
