Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Ryan Gosling live?

Ryan Gosling lives in Los Angeles with longtime partner Eva Mendes and their two daughters, Esmeralda and Amada. They're known for keeping their family life extremely private and avoiding the spotlight.

Where is Ryan Gosling from?

Ryan Gosling was born in London, Ontario, Canada. He was raised by his mother in Ontario before starting his acting career on The Mickey Mouse Club in the early 1990s. He retains Canadian citizenship and visits regularly.
