Travel Profile

Tom Brady's vacation style reflects his legendary discipline—combining relaxation with his TB12 wellness regimen. Even on vacation, he maintains strict nutrition and workout schedules.

The Bahamas, particularly Baker's Bay, has been a longtime favorite for family trips. Costa Rica appeals for its combination of beaches, surfing, and wellness culture. Post-retirement, he's been spotted at various Mediterranean and Caribbean destinations.

His Miami base provides year-round access to beaches and training facilities. Brady's travel often includes water sports, particularly surfing and paddleboarding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Tom Brady vacation?

Tom Brady's favorite vacation spots include the Bahamas (Baker's Bay), Costa Rica, and various Mediterranean destinations. He maintains his TB12 wellness routine even while traveling.

Where does Tom Brady live now?

Tom Brady currently resides in Miami, Florida, on Indian Creek Island—known as "Billionaire Bunker." He previously lived in the Boston area during his Patriots years and briefly in Tampa.
