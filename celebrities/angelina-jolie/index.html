Travel Profile

Angelina Jolie, the Oscar-winning actress and UNHCR Special Envoy, is one of the most-traveled celebrities in the world. Her work as a UN refugee advocate has taken her to over 40 countries, visiting refugee camps and conflict zones to raise awareness.

Beyond humanitarian travel, Jolie is known for cultural vacations with her six children. Cambodia holds a special place in her heart, where she owns property and has supported conservation efforts. She also has ties to France through Château Miraval and frequently visits London and various European destinations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Angelina Jolie vacation?

Angelina Jolie frequently travels to Cambodia (where she has a home), France, Italy, London, and various destinations connected to her humanitarian work. She's known for combining travel with her UN refugee advocacy.

Does Angelina Jolie own property in Cambodia?

Yes, Angelina Jolie owns a home in Cambodia, a country close to her heart since filming 'Tomb Raider' there. Cambodia is also where she adopted her son Maddox and has supported numerous conservation efforts.

What type of vacations does Angelina Jolie prefer?

Angelina Jolie prefers culturally rich destinations and often combines travel with her humanitarian work. She enjoys visiting historical sites, spending time in nature, and exploring destinations with her children.
