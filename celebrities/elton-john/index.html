Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Elton John live?

Elton John owns multiple properties: a mansion in Windsor (UK), a villa in Nice (France), a home in Beverly Hills, and property in Atlanta. He splits time between these residences with husband David Furnish and their two sons.

Where is Elton John from?

Elton John (born Reginald Kenneth Dwight) is from Pinner, Middlesex, England, in the London suburbs. He trained at the Royal Academy of Music before pursuing pop music. He was knighted by Queen Elizabeth II in 1998.
