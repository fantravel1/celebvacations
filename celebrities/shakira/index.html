Travel Profile

Shakira's life changed dramatically in 2023 when she relocated from Barcelona to Miami following her separation from soccer star Gerard Piqué after 11 years together.

Miami now serves as her primary base, offering beach lifestyle, a strong Latin community, and proximity to her Caribbean homeland. Colombia—particularly her hometown of Barranquilla—remains close to her heart for family visits.

Her two sons, Milan and Sasha, travel with her between Miami and Barcelona (where they visit their father). Beach vacations in the Caribbean are a natural extension of her Miami-based lifestyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Shakira live now?

Shakira now lives in Miami, Florida, having relocated from Barcelona in 2023 following her separation from Gerard Piqué. She purchased a mansion on Miami Beach where she lives with her two sons.

Where is Shakira from?

Shakira is from Barranquilla, Colombia. She remains deeply connected to her Colombian roots and returns frequently for family visits and to support various charitable causes in her home country.
