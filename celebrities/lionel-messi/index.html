Travel Profile

Lionel Messi's vacation patterns center on family time with childhood sweetheart Antonela Roccuzzo and their three sons—Thiago, Mateo, and Ciro. Ibiza has been the consistent summer destination for years.

His 2023 move to Inter Miami brought a major lifestyle change after decades in Barcelona and a brief Paris stint with PSG. Miami now serves as home base, offering beach access and a growing Argentine community.

Rosario, Argentina remains close to his heart—returning to his hometown to visit family and friends. Mediterranean yacht trips, often with fellow footballer friends like Luis Suárez and Cesc Fàbregas, are a summer tradition.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Lionel Messi live now?

Lionel Messi currently lives in Miami, Florida after signing with Inter Miami in 2023. He previously lived in Barcelona for over two decades and briefly in Paris during his PSG tenure.

Where does Messi vacation in summer?

Messi's favorite summer destination is Ibiza, Spain. He's been vacationing there for years, often on yacht trips with family and footballer friends including Luis Suárez, Cesc Fàbregas, and their families.
