Travel Profile

Eva Mendes is among Hollywood's most private celebrities. Since having children with Ryan Gosling, she's stepped back from acting and keeps family life away from public scrutiny, including vacations.

Her Cuban heritage (parents emigrated from Cuba) suggests cultural connection to Latin destinations. She's largely retired from acting to focus on raising her daughters Esmeralda and Amada. Business ventures (fashion line) provide some work travel.

Born in Miami, Florida and raised in Los Angeles, she rose to fame in the 2000s with films like Training Day and Hitch. Her relationship with Ryan Gosling began in 2011 during The Place Beyond the Pines.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Eva Mendes live?

Eva Mendes lives in Los Angeles with longtime partner Ryan Gosling and their two daughters, Esmeralda and Amada. They're extremely private and rarely discuss their home life publicly.

Where is Eva Mendes from?

Eva Mendes was born in Miami, Florida. Her parents emigrated from Cuba. She was raised in the Los Angeles area (Glendale and Silver Lake) after her family moved when she was young.
