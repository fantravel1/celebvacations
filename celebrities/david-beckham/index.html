David Beckham

Football Legend & Global Style Icon

$450M Net Worth
4 Children
25+ Years with Victoria

David Beckham's travel style perfectly blends British sophistication with global adventure. From his English countryside estate to Mediterranean super-yachts and his new Miami life, Beckham exemplifies the modern jet-setting lifestyle while maintaining strong family values. With Victoria and their four children, the Beckhams have become the ultimate aspirational travel family.

The Beckham Travel Style

Family First

Despite their hectic schedules, the Beckhams prioritize family vacations. Their trips often include all four children, creating lasting memories from ski slopes to yacht decks.

Transatlantic Life

Splitting time between London, the Cotswolds, and Miami, the Beckhams live a truly transatlantic existence. Private jets make the commute manageable.

Celebrity Inner Circle

Travel companions include Elton John, Gordon Ramsay, and fellow footballers. Their yacht trips often feature famous friends and family celebrations.

The Beckham Property Empire

Cotswolds Estate — England

Country Retreat

The Beckhams' beloved English countryside estate, nicknamed "Beckingham Palace II," is a converted barn on a sprawling property in the Cotswolds. The £6 million estate features a football pitch, swimming pool, and quintessentially English gardens. It's their escape from London life and where David pursues beekeeping.

Estate Features:

  • Natural swimming lake with decking
  • Full-size football pitch
  • Beehives for David's honey hobby
  • Extensive gardens and orchards
  • Guest cottage for visitors

London Townhouse

Holland Park

Their primary London residence, purchased for £31 million. The Georgian townhouse serves as their UK base, close to the children's schools and Victoria's fashion business.

Miami Penthouse

Brickell, Miami

A £20 million+ penthouse in the One Thousand Museum tower designed by Zaha Hadid. Their base for David's Inter Miami CF duties and the family's growing American life.

Signature Destinations

Miami, Florida

Second Home

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the Beckhams live?

The Beckhams split their time between three main residences: their Holland Park townhouse in London (primary UK residence), their Cotswolds country estate (weekend retreat), and their Miami penthouse (US base for Inter Miami CF).

Where do the Beckhams go on holiday?

The Beckhams favor Mediterranean yacht trips in summer (particularly Croatia, Lake Como, and the Amalfi Coast), Alpine skiing in winter, and visits to the Maldives and Dubai for beach escapes. They often vacation with celebrity friends like the Ramsays and Elton John.

What is David Beckham's net worth?

David Beckham's net worth is estimated at approximately $450 million. Combined with Victoria's fashion empire earnings, the couple's joint wealth approaches $500 million.

Does David Beckham own a yacht?

The Beckhams typically charter superyachts rather than owning outright. They've been seen on vessels like Madsummer (€1.6 million/week) and Yas (€600,000/week) during Mediterranean summers.
