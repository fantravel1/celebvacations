Travel Profile

Serena Williams' travel has evolved post-retirement from professional tennis, shifting from tournament schedules to family vacations with husband Alexis Ohanian (Reddit co-founder) and their daughters.

Her Florida bases—Miami and Palm Beach Gardens—provide year-round beach access. The Bahamas is a frequent quick escape, while European trips often combine with fashion weeks (she has her own clothing line) and the tournament cities she knows well.

Italy and France feature heavily in her travel history, both for vacation and extended stays during Grand Slam periods. The family has documented trips to the Maldives and various Caribbean destinations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Serena Williams live?

Serena Williams has properties in Florida, including Miami and Palm Beach Gardens. She lives with husband Alexis Ohanian (Reddit co-founder) and their daughters.

Where did Serena Williams get married?

Serena Williams married Alexis Ohanian in New Orleans in 2017. The star-studded wedding included many celebrity guests and was themed around "Beauty and the Beast."
