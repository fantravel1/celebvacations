Travel Profile

Travis Kelce, the record-breaking Kansas City Chiefs tight end, has become one of the most recognizable athletes in the world—both for his on-field dominance and his high-profile relationship with Taylor Swift. His travel has expanded dramatically since 2023.

Known for his fun-loving personality, Travis enjoys tropical getaways, European adventures, and attending major events worldwide. During the NFL off-season, he maximizes travel time, often jet-setting to join Taylor on her international tour stops while also maintaining his own vacation traditions with friends and family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Travis Kelce vacation?

Travis Kelce vacations in the Bahamas, Europe (particularly during Taylor Swift's Eras Tour), Lake Como, and various tropical destinations. Since dating Taylor Swift, he's been spotted at numerous international locations following her tour.

Where do Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift vacation together?

Travis and Taylor have been spotted vacationing in the Bahamas, Lake Como in Italy, and various European cities. Travis frequently travels to meet Taylor during her international Eras Tour stops.

What does Travis Kelce do in the off-season?

During the NFL off-season, Travis Kelce travels extensively, often joining Taylor Swift on tour, vacationing in tropical destinations, and spending time with family. He's also known for attending major sporting events and festivals.
