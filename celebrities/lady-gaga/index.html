Travel Profile

Lady Gaga prioritizes wellness and recovery in her travel choices, often seeking secluded destinations that allow her to recharge between intensive tours and film projects.

Her Malibu estate serves as her primary retreat, with ocean views and privacy. She's been open about managing chronic pain (fibromyalgia), which influences her vacation choices toward relaxation and healing.

Italy holds special significance for Gaga given her Italian heritage. She's been spotted in Rome, the Amalfi Coast, and various Italian locations. The Bahamas and Mexico provide tropical escapes when she seeks sun and beach time.
