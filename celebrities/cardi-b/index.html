Travel Profile

Cardi B is known for extravagant vacations documented across social media. Her Dominican heritage (her father is Dominican) means frequent trips to the Dominican Republic, while her love of luxury takes her to high-end resorts globally.

Mexico—particularly Cabo and Cancun—features regularly. Miami provides easy Caribbean access and Latin culture she embraces. Her children Kulture and Wave join family vacations, often showcased on Instagram.

Born Belcalis Marlenis Almánzar in the Bronx, New York, she rose from stripper to reality TV (Love & Hip Hop) to Grammy-winning rapper. Her Bronx roots and Caribbean heritage shape her travel preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Cardi B live?

Cardi B has properties in the New York area and in Atlanta (where she previously lived with Offset). She maintains strong New York connections while also having an LA presence for music industry work.

Where is Cardi B from?

Cardi B (Belcalis Marlenis Almánzar) was born and raised in the Bronx, New York. Her father is Dominican and her mother is Trinidadian. She grew up in the Highbridge neighborhood and spent summers in Washington Heights.
