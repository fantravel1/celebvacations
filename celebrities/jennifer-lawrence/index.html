Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Jennifer Lawrence live?

Jennifer Lawrence lives primarily in New York City with husband Cooke Maroney and their son. She also owns property in Los Angeles. She's maintained bi-coastal living throughout her career.

Where is Jennifer Lawrence from?

Jennifer Lawrence was born and raised in Louisville, Kentucky. She grew up in the Indian Hills neighborhood and was discovered at age 14 during a family trip to New York City. She maintains close ties to her Kentucky roots.
