Travel Profile

The Weeknd (Abel Tesfaye) maintains one of the most private personal lives among major artists, which extends to his vacation patterns. His Los Angeles estate serves as his primary retreat.

Born in Toronto, he maintains Canadian connections while basing himself in LA's Bel Air neighborhood. His humanitarian work with the UN World Food Programme has taken him to Ethiopia, connecting with his Ethiopian heritage.

When he does vacation, Caribbean destinations and Italy have appeared in limited documentation. He prefers privacy over the highly-photographed vacation style of some peers.
