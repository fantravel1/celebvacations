Travel Profile

Matt Damon maintains a notably grounded family life despite A-list status. His vacation style prioritizes quality time with wife Luciana Barroso and their four daughters over celebrity hotspots.

Australia—particularly Byron Bay—became a significant destination during the COVID-19 pandemic, when the family relocated there temporarily. The Hemsworth family connection (they're friends and Byron Bay neighbors of Chris Hemsworth) adds to the appeal.

Born in Cambridge, Massachusetts, he maintains Boston roots despite living primarily in Los Angeles and Brooklyn. His friendship with Ben Affleck often results in joint family vacations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Matt Damon live?

Matt Damon splits time between Los Angeles (Pacific Palisades) and Brooklyn, New York. He's lived in LA for years and also maintains East Coast property. Born in Cambridge, Massachusetts, he keeps Boston connections.

Did Matt Damon move to Australia?

Matt Damon and his family spent extended time in Byron Bay, Australia during 2020-2021, prompting speculation about a permanent move. While they haven't officially relocated, Australia remains a favorite destination, partly due to his friendship with Chris Hemsworth who lives there.
