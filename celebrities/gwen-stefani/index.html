Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Gwen Stefani live?

Gwen Stefani lives primarily in Encino, Los Angeles with husband Blake Shelton and her three sons. They also spend significant time at Blake's ranch in Tishomingo, Oklahoma, dividing time between California and country life.

Where is Gwen Stefani from?

Gwen Stefani was born and raised in Fullerton, California (Orange County). She formed No Doubt while in high school with friends. Her Orange County roots influenced the ska-punk sound of early No Doubt music.
