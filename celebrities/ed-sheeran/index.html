Travel Profile

Ed Sheeran bucks the celebrity trend of jet-setting between glamorous hotspots. His Suffolk estate "Sheeranville" has grown into a private compound that serves as his primary retreat from touring life.

The English countryside estate includes multiple properties, a pub, a treehouse, a recording studio, and even a private chapel where he married Cherry Seaborn. He's invested heavily in creating a self-contained haven.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Ed Sheeran live?

Ed Sheeran lives on a sprawling estate in Suffolk, England, which fans have nicknamed "Sheeranville." He's purchased multiple adjacent properties over time, creating a compound that includes his home, a private pub, a treehouse, a chapel, and a recording studio.

Is Ed Sheeran married?

Yes, Ed Sheeran married Cherry Seaborn in 2019 at the chapel on his Suffolk estate. They have two daughters together. Cherry is his childhood sweetheart from their school days in Suffolk.
