Kylie Jenner

Beauty Mogul & Travel Influencer

$700M+ Net Worth
400M+ Instagram Followers
2 Children

Kylie Jenner has turned travel into content and content into billions. As the youngest self-made billionaire (per Forbes' initial assessment) and the most-followed Kardashian-Jenner on social media, her vacation posts generate millions of engagements and have the power to put destinations on the map. Her travel style is unapologetically extravagant: private jets, mega-yachts, and villa rentals that cost more than most homes.

Property Portfolio

Holmby Hills Compound

Los Angeles, California

Primary residence purchased for $36.5 million in 2020. The 15,000-square-foot estate includes a resort-style pool, guest house, and sprawling grounds in one of LA's most exclusive neighborhoods.

Hidden Hills Estate

Hidden Hills, California

Previously her main home, the Hidden Hills property keeps her close to the Kardashian-Jenner family compound. Multiple properties in the gated community.

The Kylie Travel Experience

Private Aviation

Kylie's custom Global Express private jet features pink interior, mood lighting, and "Kylie" branding. The jet became controversial for short flights but remains her primary transport mode. Estimated cost: $72 million.

Mega-Yacht Charters

Birthday celebrations aboard superyachts have become tradition. Her 22nd birthday was celebrated on the $250 million Tranquility yacht. Mediterranean summer trips regularly feature 300+ foot vessels.

Content-First Travel

Every trip produces carefully curated content. Bikini shots, sunset moments, and family scenes generate millions in engagement value. Her vacation posts are among Instagram's most-liked.

Birthday Trips

Superyacht Tranquility along the Italian and French coasts. The most documented celebrity birthday trip in Instagram history. Custom decorations, matching outfits for the friend group, and non-stop content.

25th Birthday (2022)

Turks and Caicos villa takeover with family and friends. Private beach access, personal chef, and coordinated swimwear looks.

Family Travel

With Stormi & Aire

Since becoming a mother, Kylie's travel has evolved to include family-friendly elements while maintaining luxury standards. Stormi's first trip to the Bahamas went viral. Both children now feature in carefully selected vacation content.

Kardashian-Jenner Group Trips

Annual family vacations involve complex logistics: multiple security teams, nannies, glam squads, and content teams. Costa Rica, Bali, and various Caribbean islands have hosted the full family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Kylie Jenner vacation?

Kylie Jenner's most frequent vacation destinations include Turks and Caicos, the Italian coast (Amalfi, Portofino), the Bahamas, Aspen for skiing, and Paris for fashion week. She favors private villas, mega-yacht charters, and ultra-exclusive resorts that guarantee privacy.

Does Kylie Jenner have a private jet?

Yes, Kylie owns a custom Bombardier Global Express 7500 valued at approximately $72 million. The jet features a custom pink interior, "Kylie" branding, and luxury amenities. She uses it for both domestic and international travel.

What hotels does Kylie Jenner stay at?

Kylie frequently stays at the Ritz Paris, The Mark in New York City, Amanyara in Turks and Caicos, and various luxury resorts in the Bahamas. For extended stays, she prefers private villa rentals that can accommodate her family and security team.
