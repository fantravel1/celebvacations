Travel Profile

Cindy Crawford, one of the original supermodels, has built a lifestyle empire with husband Rande Gerber that extends into hospitality. Their Casamigos tequila venture (sold for $1 billion) with George Clooney has opened doors to exclusive destinations worldwide.

The Crawford-Gerber family maintains homes in Malibu and Cabo San Lucas, creating a bi-coastal beach lifestyle. Their close friendship with George and Amal Clooney means they're frequently spotted together at Lake Como and other exclusive destinations.

Where does Cindy Crawford vacation?

Cindy Crawford vacations in Malibu, Mexico (especially Cabo San Lucas where she and Rande Gerber have a home), St. Barts, and travels frequently with longtime friends George and Amal Clooney. Her travel often combines family time with business ventures in hospitality.
