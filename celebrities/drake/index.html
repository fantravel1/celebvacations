Drake

The 6 God's Global Lifestyle

$250M+ Net Worth
$100M Toronto Mansion
50K+ Sq Ft Home

Drake doesn't just travel—he transforms destinations into cultural moments. From his record-breaking Toronto mansion (featured in Architectural Digest) to Turks and Caicos beach escapes, the rapper's travel choices reflect his dual nature: deeply rooted in Toronto yet drawn to tropical luxury. His Instagram, @champagnepapi, has turned vacation spots into trending destinations overnight.

The Embassy — Toronto

Drake's $100 million Toronto mansion, dubbed "The Embassy," is one of the most extravagant celebrity homes on earth. Designed by Ferris Rafauli and featured in Architectural Digest, the 50,000-square-foot Bridle Path estate is a monument to excess done right.

NBA-Regulation Court

A full-size indoor basketball court with the OVO owl logo at center court. Used for celebrity games and private workouts.

The Great Room

44-foot ceilings, a concert-quality grand piano, and bespoke limestone. Designed for entertaining on a scale few homes can match.

Award Room

A dedicated room displaying Grammy awards, platinum records, and memorabilia. Jersey collection includes Michael Jordan, LeBron James, and Kobe Bryant originals.

Travel Style

Private Aviation

Drake's custom Boeing 767, "Air Drake," is one of the most lavish private planes in the world. Custom OVO branding, multiple lounges, and bedroom suites. A flying mansion that eliminates the need for hotel stays on tour.

Yacht Life

Regular yacht charters in the Caribbean and Mediterranean. Spotted on superyachts in Turks and Caicos, St. Barts, and European waters. Prefers vessels with recording studio capabilities.

Casino Culture

Known for high-stakes gambling, Drake has made headlines with massive wins and losses. Regular at top casinos and has streamed gambling sessions with millions watching.

Courtside Everywhere

NBA obsession means travel follows basketball. Courtside at Raptors games in Toronto, Lakers in LA, and marquee matchups across the country. The NBA's most famous fan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Drake live?

Drake's primary residence is "The Embassy," a 50,000-square-foot mansion in Toronto's Bridle Path neighborhood, valued at over $100 million. He also maintains properties in Hidden Hills, California. The Toronto home was featured in Architectural Digest and designed by Ferris Rafauli.

Where does Drake vacation?

Drake frequently vacations in Turks and Caicos, Barbados, and various Mediterranean destinations. He's known for yacht trips in the Caribbean and European waters. Miami is a regular destination for nightlife and basketball.

Does Drake have a private jet?

Yes, Drake owns a custom Boeing 767 known as "Air Drake." The plane features OVO branding, multiple lounges, bedroom suites, and luxury amenities. It's one of the most expensive celebrity private aircraft.
