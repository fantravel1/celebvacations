Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Patrick Mahomes live?

Patrick Mahomes lives in the Kansas City area with wife Brittany and their children. He signed a record-breaking contract with the Chiefs and has become synonymous with the Kansas City community through both football and business ventures.

Where is Patrick Mahomes from?

Patrick Mahomes was born in Tyler, Texas. His father Pat Mahomes Sr. was a professional baseball pitcher. He played college football at Texas Tech University before being drafted by the Kansas City Chiefs in 2017.
