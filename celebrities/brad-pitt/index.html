Travel Profile

Brad Pitt's Château Miraval in Provence, France represents both a business venture (the acclaimed Miraval rosé) and a personal retreat. The estate became central to legal disputes following his divorce from Angelina Jolie.

His passion for architecture influences travel—he's associated with sustainable building and design projects. Art collecting also takes him to galleries and auctions worldwide. Los Angeles remains his primary residence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Brad Pitt own a vineyard?

Yes, Brad Pitt owns Château Miraval, a wine estate in Provence, France. The estate produces the acclaimed Miraval rosé. He purchased it with former wife Angelina Jolie in 2008; the property became subject to legal disputes following their divorce.

Where is Brad Pitt from?

Brad Pitt was born in Shawnee, Oklahoma and raised in Springfield, Missouri. He moved to Los Angeles to pursue acting after leaving the University of Missouri just before graduation.
