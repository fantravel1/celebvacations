Travel Profile

Dua Lipa has become synonymous with European summer style, frequently documented on Mediterranean yachts and beach destinations. Her Albanian heritage influences regular visits to the Balkans.

Ibiza is a regular summer destination, aligning with her dance-pop aesthetic and love of nightlife. Italy—particularly Capri, Positano, and Portofino—appears frequently in her travel content, as does the French Riviera.

Albania remains close to her heart; she visits family in Kosovo and has promoted Albanian tourism. London serves as her home base between worldwide tours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Dua Lipa from?

Dua Lipa was born in London to Albanian parents from Kosovo. She spent part of her childhood in Kosovo before returning to London. She maintains strong ties to her Albanian heritage.

Where does Dua Lipa vacation in summer?

Dua Lipa's summer vacations typically include Mediterranean destinations: Ibiza, the Italian coast (Capri, Amalfi), St. Tropez, and Greek islands. She's frequently photographed on yachts and at beach clubs.
