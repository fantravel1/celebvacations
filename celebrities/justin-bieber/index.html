Justin Bieber

Pop Superstar & World Traveler

$300M+ Net Worth
295M+ Instagram Followers
2018 Married Hailey

Justin Bieber's travel evolution mirrors his personal transformation. From the wild early-career exploits to today's more grounded lifestyle with wife Hailey Baldwin Bieber, his vacation choices now emphasize wellness, privacy, and quality time. The Biebers have become one of pop culture's most watched couples, and their travel content—from Caribbean beaches to Italian villas—generates millions of engagements.

The Bieber Properties

Beverly Hills Mansion

Los Angeles, California

Their primary LA residence, purchased for $25.8 million in 2020. The 13,000-square-foot estate in Beverly Park includes tennis court, massive pool, and private grounds. Previously owned by Madonna.

Ontario Lake House

Ontario, Canada

Justin maintains strong Canadian roots with lakeside property. The retreat offers escape from LA and connection to his hometown origins. Documented visits show relaxed family time.

Bieber Travel Style

Private Aviation

Like most A-list artists, the Biebers travel by private jet for most trips. Tour schedules demand flexibility that commercial travel can't provide. The jet becomes an extension of home.

Yacht Life

Mediterranean and Caribbean yacht charters are frequent. The privacy of being at sea suits their desire to avoid crowds. Superyacht trips often include celebrity friends.

Wellness Focus

Hailey's wellness interests influence their destinations. Healthy restaurants, yoga-friendly hotels, and destinations with wellness offerings. A contrast to Justin's earlier party-focused travel.

With Hailey

Romantic Getaways

Work Travel Together

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Justin Bieber live?

Justin Bieber's primary residence is a $25.8 million mansion in Beverly Park, Los Angeles (previously owned by Madonna). He also maintains property in Ontario, Canada, connecting him to his hometown roots. The Beverly Hills estate spans 13,000 square feet.

Where do Justin and Hailey Bieber vacation?

The Biebers frequently vacation in Turks and Caicos, Hawaii, Greece (yacht trips), Italy (Lake Como, Amalfi Coast), and Japan. They prefer destinations offering privacy, luxury accommodations, and beautiful beaches. Caribbean escapes and Mediterranean summers are recurring themes.

Does Justin Bieber have a private jet?

Justin Bieber primarily travels by private aviation, as is standard for A-list artists with demanding tour schedules. Whether owning or chartering, private jets provide the flexibility, privacy, and convenience essential for his lifestyle.
