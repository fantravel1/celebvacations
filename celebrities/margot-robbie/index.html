Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Margot Robbie from?

Margot Robbie is from Dalby, Queensland, Australia, but grew up on the Gold Coast. She got her start on the Australian soap opera Neighbours before moving to Hollywood.

Where did Margot Robbie get married?

Margot Robbie married producer Tom Ackerley in a secret ceremony in Byron Bay, Australia in December 2016. They run LuckyChap Entertainment together, producing films like Barbie.
