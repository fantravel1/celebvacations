Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Mark Wahlberg live now?

Mark Wahlberg moved from Los Angeles to Las Vegas, Nevada in 2022. He cited business opportunities (including a movie studio project) and wanting a better environment for his children as reasons for the move. He built a large estate in the Las Vegas area.

Where is Mark Wahlberg from?

Mark Wahlberg was born and raised in Dorchester, Boston, Massachusetts. He's the youngest of nine children and grew up in a working-class Irish-American Catholic family. He maintains strong Boston ties through his Wahlburgers restaurant chain and frequent visits.
