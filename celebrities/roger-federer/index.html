Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Roger Federer live?

Roger Federer lives in Switzerland with his wife Mirka and their four children. He has property in the Zurich area and reportedly also has a home in Valbella in the Swiss Alps. He's long been a resident of Switzerland and is one of the country's most famous citizens.

Where is Roger Federer from?

Roger Federer was born in Basel, Switzerland. His mother Lynette is South African, giving him dual heritage. He grew up in the Basel area and played at the Swiss tennis academy system before turning professional.
