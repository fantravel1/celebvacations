Frequently Asked Questions

Does Michael Jordan own a yacht?

Yes, Michael Jordan owns "Catch 23," a 230-foot superyacht valued at $80 million. The yacht features a basketball court on deck and is frequently seen in Florida and Bahamas waters.

Where does Michael Jordan play golf?

Jordan is a member at numerous exclusive clubs. He built a private course at his Jupiter, Florida home and frequents destinations like Pebble Beach, Scottsdale, and various private clubs. He's known for his competitive approach to golf.
