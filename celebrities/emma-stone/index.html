Travel Profile

Emma Stone is notably private for an A-list actress. She reportedly relocated from Los Angeles to Austin, Texas with husband Dave McCary (SNL director/writer), seeking a more grounded lifestyle for their daughter.

European film festivals (Cannes, Venice) are annual fixtures for her award-caliber work. Her working relationship with director Yorgos Lanthimos has taken her to Greece and various European locations for filming.

Born Emily Jean Stone in Scottsdale, Arizona, she moved to Los Angeles as a teenager to pursue acting. Her Arizona roots may influence appreciation for quieter, non-Hollywood living.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Emma Stone live?

Emma Stone reportedly lives in Austin, Texas with husband Dave McCary and their daughter. She previously lived in Los Angeles and New York. The Austin move reflects her preference for privacy away from Hollywood.

Where is Emma Stone from?

Emma Stone (born Emily Jean Stone) is from Scottsdale, Arizona. She was raised there before convincing her parents to let her move to Los Angeles as a teenager to pursue acting. She got her breakthrough on TV before transitioning to film.
