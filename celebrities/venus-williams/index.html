Travel Profile

Venus Williams' travel is shaped by both the professional tennis tour and her business ventures. Her interior design firm V Starr and fashion brand EleVen keep her traveling beyond tennis obligations.

Miami is home base, providing year-round warm weather and easy access to both Caribbean vacation spots and international flights. The tennis tour takes her to Grand Slam cities: Melbourne, Paris, London, and New York annually.

Born in Lynwood, California and raised partly in Compton, she trained in Florida from a young age. Her partnership with sister Serena means family travel is intertwined with professional obligations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Venus Williams live?

Venus Williams lives in the Palm Beach Gardens area of Florida. She's had property in the Miami/South Florida area for years, which provides easy access to training facilities and a warm climate year-round.

Where is Venus Williams from?

Venus Williams was born in Lynwood, California and grew up partly in Compton. Her family moved to Florida when she was young so she and sister Serena could train at Rick Macci's tennis academy. She's closely associated with both California and Florida.
