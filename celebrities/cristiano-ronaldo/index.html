Travel Profile

Cristiano Ronaldo is one of the most traveled athletes in the world, combining football commitments with luxury family vacations on his private yacht and at exclusive resorts worldwide.

His Azimut Grande yacht is frequently used for Mediterranean cruises, particularly around Ibiza, the French Riviera, and Sardinia. Dubai has become a second home, where he owns property and is frequently spotted during breaks.

Madeira, Portugal—his birthplace—remains a consistent destination for family visits. Ronaldo maintains his legendary fitness even on vacation, often photographed working out at hotel gyms worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Cristiano Ronaldo vacation?

Ronaldo's favorite vacation destinations include Dubai (where he owns property), Ibiza, Madeira (his birthplace), Mykonos, the Maldives, and the French Riviera. He often uses his private yacht for Mediterranean trips.

Does Cristiano Ronaldo own a yacht?

Yes, Ronaldo owns an Azimut Grande yacht valued at approximately $7 million. He's frequently photographed aboard with his family in Mediterranean destinations.
