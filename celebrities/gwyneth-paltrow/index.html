Travel Profile

Gwyneth Paltrow, Oscar-winning actress and founder of lifestyle brand Goop, has arguably influenced modern wellness travel more than any other celebrity. Her travel choices often become trends—destinations and experiences she endorses through Goop frequently see spikes in bookings.

Her travel style combines luxury with wellness, seeking out the world's best spas, clean-eating destinations, and experiences that align with the Goop philosophy of conscious living. She maintains homes in LA and the Hamptons, using both as bases for different seasons.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Gwyneth Paltrow vacation?

Gwyneth Paltrow vacations in the Hamptons, Mexico, European destinations including Italy and Spain, and wellness retreats worldwide. As founder of Goop, she often combines travel with wellness experiences and has homes in multiple locations.
