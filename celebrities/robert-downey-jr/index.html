Travel Profile

Robert Downey Jr's post-Marvel life has focused on family time with wife Susan (who is also his producing partner) and their two children, along with environmental advocacy through his Footprint Coalition.

His real estate portfolio includes a Malibu windmill home (converted to an eco-friendly residence), Hamptons property in East Hampton, and farmland. The windmill house reflects his environmental interests.

His wellness-focused lifestyle (he practices Wing Chun kung fu and maintains a healthy routine after his well-documented recovery) influences travel choices toward restorative destinations rather than party scenes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Robert Downey Jr live?

Robert Downey Jr's primary residence is his famous windmill house in Malibu, California, which he and wife Susan converted into an eco-friendly home. He also owns property in the Hamptons (East Hampton, NY) and farmland.

What is Robert Downey Jr's Footprint Coalition?

The Footprint Coalition is Robert Downey Jr's environmental organization focused on using advanced technology to address environmental challenges. His environmental interests extend to his lifestyle, including his eco-friendly home and sustainable practices.
