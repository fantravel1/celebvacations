Travel Profile

Chris Pratt balances blockbuster Hollywood with ranch life. He owns property including farmland where he raises sheep, and his vacation style leans toward outdoor activities and family time over glitzy destinations.

Hawaii provides beach family vacations. His ranch properties offer "staycation" retreats from LA life. His marriage to Katherine Schwarzenegger (daughter of Arnold Schwarzenegger) connects him to Kennedy family vacation spots.

Born in Virginia, Minnesota, he was living in Hawaii (homeless, living in a van) when discovered. He rose to fame on Parks and Recreation before becoming a blockbuster star with Guardians of the Galaxy and Jurassic World.
