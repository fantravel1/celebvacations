Travel Profile

Adele maintains strong privacy around her personal life, which extends to her vacation habits. Her Las Vegas residency at Caesars Palace has largely kept her stateside in recent years.

Her relationship with sports agent Rich Paul (who represents LeBron James) means attending NBA games and sports events. Caribbean vacations have been documented, notably during the period of her well-publicized fitness transformation.

Born in Tottenham, London, she maintains a London property alongside her primary LA base. Italy has been a documented vacation destination. Her son Angelo (from her marriage to Simon Konecki) travels with her.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Adele live?

Adele primarily lives in Los Angeles with partner Rich Paul. She purchased a Beverly Hills mansion and also owns property in her native London. Her Las Vegas residency keeps her traveling between LA and Vegas frequently.

Where is Adele from?

Adele is from Tottenham, North London, England. She maintains her British identity and accent despite living primarily in the United States for years. She keeps property in London and visits regularly.
