Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Chrissy Teigen live?

Chrissy Teigen lives in Beverly Hills, California with husband John Legend and their children. The family has owned multiple properties in the Los Angeles area, including a previous home in the Hollywood Hills.

Where is Chrissy Teigen from?

Chrissy Teigen was born in Delta, Utah. Her mother is Thai (from Thailand) and her father is of Norwegian descent. She moved frequently as a child before the family settled in California. Her Thai heritage strongly influences her food and travel interests.
