Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Chris Evans live?

Chris Evans lives primarily in Los Angeles for his film career, but he's maintained strong ties to his hometown of Boston, Massachusetts. He's known for his love of New England and visits family there frequently.

Where is Chris Evans from?

Chris Evans was born in Boston, Massachusetts and raised in Sudbury, a suburb west of Boston. His Irish-Italian family is large and close-knit. He's a huge Patriots fan and Boston sports supporter.
