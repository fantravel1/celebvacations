Travel Profile

Mariah Carey, the five-octave voice behind "All I Want for Christmas Is You," brings her signature glamour to every vacation. Known for never being photographed in unflattering circumstances, her travel style is unapologetically luxurious and theatrical.

Her annual Aspen Christmas tradition has become pop culture legend—footage of Mariah in the snow, in stilettos, sipping champagne has defined her holiday persona. She's equally drawn to summer Mediterranean escapes and exclusive Caribbean retreats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Mariah Carey vacation?

Mariah Carey vacations in Aspen (her legendary Christmas destination), St. Barts for New Year's, Capri in summer, and luxury resorts worldwide. She's known for her extravagant vacation style and love of winter destinations.

Where does Mariah Carey spend Christmas?

Mariah Carey famously spends Christmas in Aspen, Colorado almost every year. Her annual Aspen visits have become as legendary as her holiday song, featuring elaborate celebrations at luxury properties like The Little Nell.
