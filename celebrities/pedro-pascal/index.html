Travel Profile

Pedro Pascal, the Chilean-American actor who has captured hearts as Din Djarin in "The Mandalorian" and Joel in "The Last of Us," is known for his warm personality and connection to his roots. His travel reflects both his demanding career and his desire to stay connected to family and heritage.

Unlike many Hollywood stars who seek tropical escapes, Pedro often gravitates toward culturally rich destinations and cities with vibrant arts scenes. He's frequently spotted in New York City, where he maintains a residence, and makes regular trips to Chile to visit family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Pedro Pascal vacation?

Pedro Pascal frequently visits Chile (his homeland), Spain, New York City, and various European destinations. He maintains strong connections to his Chilean roots and often returns to South America.

Where is Pedro Pascal from?

Pedro Pascal was born in Santiago, Chile, and his family fled to the United States when he was young. He maintains close ties to Chile and visits regularly.

What cities does Pedro Pascal spend time in?

Pedro Pascal splits his time between Los Angeles (for work), New York City (where he has a residence), and frequently travels to Chile and European destinations for both work and leisure.
