Travel Profile

Vin Diesel's travel centers on family and his Latin connections through longtime partner Paloma Jiménez (Mexican model). The Dominican Republic holds special significance—he's reportedly owned property there and spent significant time on the island.

The Fast & Furious franchise has taken him to film locations worldwide—from Tokyo to Rio de Janeiro to London. Miami is a frequent spot, connecting to the franchise's early films and Latin culture he embraces.

Born Mark Sinclair in New York City, he was raised in Greenwich Village. His ethnic background is mixed—he's never met his biological father. His connection to the late Paul Walker influences continued family-focused travel philosophy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Vin Diesel live?

Vin Diesel lives primarily in Los Angeles with his longtime partner Paloma Jiménez and their three children: Hania, Vincent, and Pauline (named after Paul Walker). He's also been connected to property in the Dominican Republic.

Where is Vin Diesel from?

Vin Diesel (born Mark Sinclair) is from New York City, specifically raised in Greenwich Village by his mother and stepfather. He never met his biological father and has described his ethnic background as ambiguous, which he says helped him in his acting career.
