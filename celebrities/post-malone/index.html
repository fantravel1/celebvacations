Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Post Malone live?

Post Malone lives on a large compound in Utah. He relocated from Texas and Los Angeles seeking a more private, rural lifestyle. The property includes space for his various hobbies and provides distance from the Hollywood scene.

Why does Post Malone live in Utah?

Post Malone chose Utah for its privacy, open spaces, and distance from typical celebrity hotspots. He's spoken about preferring a quieter lifestyle where he can enjoy hobbies like shooting, ATVs, and gaming without constant paparazzi attention.
