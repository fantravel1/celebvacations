Travel Profile

Kate Hudson, Oscar-nominated actress and founder of Fabletics, comes from Hollywood royalty—her mother is Goldie Hawn and she was raised by Kurt Russell. This family connection defines much of her travel, particularly to Aspen where Goldie and Kurt have been fixtures for decades.

Kate balances wellness-focused getaways with family adventures. As a mother of three and a fitness entrepreneur, she often combines travel with wellness retreats and active outdoor destinations.

Where does Kate Hudson vacation?

Kate Hudson vacations frequently in Aspen (her mother Goldie Hawn's longtime home), Greek islands in summer, and enjoys tropical beach destinations. She often travels with her extended family including mother Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell.
