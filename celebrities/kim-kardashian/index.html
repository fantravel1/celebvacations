Travel Profile

Kim Kardashian has documented her travels more thoroughly than perhaps any celebrity in history. From Instagram posts to reality TV footage, her vacations have become cultural touchstones that influence travel trends globally.

Based in Los Angeles, Kim maintains an extensive travel schedule that balances business obligations with family vacations. Paris Fashion Week is sacrosanct—she attends twice yearly without fail. Cabo San Lucas has become her go-to quick escape, while more elaborate trips to destinations like Japan and Italy often become documented family experiences.

Since becoming a mother of four, Kim's travel style has evolved to be more family-oriented, though she still maintains her fashion-forward approach. Trips often involve multiple generations of the Kardashian-Jenner family, and destinations are chosen with both luxury amenities and child-friendliness in mind.
