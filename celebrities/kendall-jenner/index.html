Travel Profile

Kendall Jenner combines model travel with leisure trips, frequently jet-setting between fashion weeks and vacation destinations with her famous model squad.

Her annual Aspen ski trips during the holidays have become tradition with the Kardashian-Jenner family. Summer brings Mediterranean yacht trips and Greek island parties in Mykonos with friends like Hailey Bieber and Bella Hadid.

Coachella is a major event on her calendar, often staying at desert compounds near the festival. Mexico destinations like Cabo San Lucas and Punta Mita are frequent quick getaways from her Los Angeles base.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Kendall Jenner ski?

Kendall Jenner's primary ski destination is Aspen, Colorado, where the Kardashian-Jenner family has made annual holiday trips a tradition. They typically stay at luxury properties and are often spotted at local restaurants and slopes.

Where does Kendall Jenner go for Coachella?

During Coachella, Kendall stays at private desert estates near the festival in La Quinta or Indian Wells. She's known for attending brand parties like Revolve Festival and posting fashion content from the event.
