Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Naomi Campbell live?

Naomi Campbell maintains homes in London (her birthplace), New York, and Kenya (her famous eco-house). She's a truly global citizen who travels constantly for fashion and philanthropy work.

Where is Naomi Campbell from?

Naomi Campbell was born in Streatham, South London, England. Her mother was a Jamaican-born dancer. She was discovered at 15 and became one of the most famous supermodels of all time, along with Cindy Crawford, Linda Evangelista, Christy Turlington, and Claudia Schiffer.
