Travel Profile

Julia Roberts has successfully maintained a private family life despite being one of Hollywood's biggest stars. Her travel prioritizes family time with cinematographer husband Danny Moder and their three children.

Hawaii has been a significant destination—she's owned property on the islands. Her Malibu home provides beach access without leaving California. A ranch in Taos, New Mexico offers a complete retreat from Hollywood.

Born in Smyrna, Georgia, she maintains some Southern connections. Film shoots take her worldwide, but vacation time focuses on privacy and normalcy for her children.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Julia Roberts live?

Julia Roberts has multiple properties including a home in Malibu, California, property in Hawaii, and a ranch in Taos, New Mexico. She lives with husband Danny Moder and their three children—twins Hazel and Phinnaeus, and son Henry.

Where is Julia Roberts from?

Julia Roberts was born in Smyrna, Georgia, a suburb of Atlanta. She grew up in Georgia before moving to New York to pursue acting, eventually settling in Los Angeles. Her siblings Eric Roberts and Lisa Roberts Gillan are also actors.
