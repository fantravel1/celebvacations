Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Gigi Hadid live?

Gigi Hadid primarily lives in New York City, where she has a SoHo apartment. She also spends time at her family's farm in Pennsylvania and in Los Angeles where she grew up. Her modeling career requires extensive travel to fashion capitals.

Where is Gigi Hadid from?

Gigi Hadid was born in Los Angeles, California. Her father Mohamed Hadid is Palestinian-American and her mother Yolanda Hadid is Dutch-American. She grew up in LA before moving to New York to pursue modeling full-time.
