Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Billie Eilish from?

Billie Eilish was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, specifically in the Highland Park neighborhood. She famously lived in her childhood home well into her career, recording her debut album in her brother Finneas's bedroom.

Where does Billie Eilish live now?

Billie Eilish purchased her own home in the Glendale area of Los Angeles. She's known for being a homebody and preferring LA over constant travel despite her global fame.
