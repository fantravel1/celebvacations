Travel Profile

Nicole Kidman lives a bi-continental life, splitting time between Nashville (husband Keith Urban's country music base) and her native Australia. This dual residency shapes her travel patterns significantly.

Sydney remains home—she grew up there and maintains strong family connections and property. Australian beaches, particularly around Sydney's Northern Beaches, are frequent escapes. Fiji has become a family vacation favorite.

Film work takes her globally—she's been photographed in Hong Kong, London, and various European locations for productions. Her daughters Sunday Rose and Faith Margaret travel with the family when schedules allow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Nicole Kidman live?

Nicole Kidman splits her time between Nashville, Tennessee (with husband Keith Urban) and Sydney, Australia. Nashville is their primary US base, while she maintains strong connections and property in her native Australia.

Where is Nicole Kidman from?

Nicole Kidman was born in Honolulu, Hawaii but raised in Sydney, Australia. She considers herself Australian and maintains dual citizenship. Her parents were Australian and the family returned to Sydney when she was young.
