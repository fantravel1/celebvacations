Travel Profile

LeBron James maximizes his NBA off-season with family vacations that combine luxury relaxation with his legendary workout regime. He's famous for maintaining elite fitness even while on vacation.

Anguilla has become LeBron's go-to Caribbean destination, with annual visits to the quiet island's luxury resorts. The Italian coast—particularly Portofino and the Amalfi Coast—draws him for Mediterranean yacht trips with wife Savannah and their children.

LeBron's social media often showcases vacation workouts, showing fans that even "King James" doesn't take days off. His travel combines family beach time with intensive training sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does LeBron James vacation?

LeBron's favorite destinations include Anguilla (his go-to Caribbean spot), Italy (Portofino, Amalfi), the Bahamas, and various Mediterranean locations. He typically takes family trips during the NBA off-season.

Does LeBron James work out on vacation?

Yes, LeBron is famous for maintaining his workout regime even on vacation. He's frequently shared workout videos from luxury hotels and yacht gyms, showing his dedication to fitness year-round.
