Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Nick Jonas live?

Nick Jonas lives in Los Angeles (Encino area) with wife Priyanka Chopra and daughter Malti Marie. They also have property in London and spend time in Mumbai for Priyanka's Bollywood work.

Where is Nick Jonas from?

Nick Jonas was born in Dallas, Texas but grew up primarily in Wyckoff, New Jersey. He was raised with brothers Kevin and Joe, who later formed the Jonas Brothers. The family is religious (their father was a pastor).
