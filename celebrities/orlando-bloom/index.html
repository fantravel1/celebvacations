Travel Profile

Orlando Bloom, the British actor famous for "Pirates of the Caribbean" and "Lord of the Rings," is known for his love of adventure and outdoor activities. He and his fiancée, pop star Katy Perry, split their time between Los Angeles and Hawaii with their daughter Daisy.

Bloom is frequently photographed surfing, paddleboarding, and enjoying outdoor adventures. His travels often feature water sports destinations and mountain retreats for skiing. The couple also enjoys European getaways, particularly in Italy and the Mediterranean.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Orlando Bloom vacation?

Orlando Bloom frequently vacations in Hawaii (where he has a home with Katy Perry), the Caribbean, Italy, Aspen for skiing, and various European destinations. He's known for adventure travel and outdoor activities.

Does Orlando Bloom live in Hawaii?

Yes, Orlando Bloom and his fiancée Katy Perry own property in Hawaii, where they spend significant time with their daughter Daisy. They've been photographed frequently enjoying the island lifestyle.

What activities does Orlando Bloom enjoy on vacation?

Orlando Bloom enjoys surfing, paddleboarding, hiking, skiing, and other outdoor adventures during his vacations. He's known for his active lifestyle and love of water sports, particularly in Hawaii.
