Travel Profile

Reese Witherspoon embodies Southern charm with her Nashville roots while running a media empire from both coasts. Her travel balances Hollywood obligations with genuine love for her Tennessee home.

Nashville isn't just hometown visits—she has substantial property there and her Draper James lifestyle brand celebrates Southern culture. Beach vacations in Hawaii and the Bahamas provide family escape time.

Her production company Hello Sunshine (sold for $900 million) has added business travel to her schedule. She travels with her three children—Ava and Deacon (with ex-husband Ryan Phillippe) and Tennessee (with husband Jim Toth, though they separated in 2023).

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Reese Witherspoon from?

Reese Witherspoon was born in New Orleans, Louisiana but grew up in Nashville, Tennessee. She maintains strong ties to Nashville, owning property there and basing her Draper James lifestyle brand on Southern culture.

Does Reese Witherspoon live in Nashville?

Reese Witherspoon has property in both Los Angeles and Nashville. While LA serves as her primary base for work, she frequently visits Nashville and has spoken extensively about her love for her Southern roots.
