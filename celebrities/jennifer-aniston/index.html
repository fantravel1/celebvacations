Jennifer Aniston

Wellness-Focused Luxury Travel & Timeless Escapes

30+ Years in Spotlight
$320M Net Worth
5 Favorite Destinations

Jennifer Aniston has mastered the art of the wellness vacation. Known for her ageless appearance and commitment to self-care, her travel choices reflect a sophisticated blend of relaxation, privacy, and luxury. From the beaches of Cabo San Lucas to the tranquil Greek islands, Aniston gravitates toward destinations that offer world-class spa facilities and secluded retreats.

Travel Philosophy

Wellness First

Every destination Aniston chooses features top-tier spa facilities. She's particularly devoted to yoga retreats and properties offering holistic wellness programs.

Privacy Priority

Prefers boutique properties and private villas over mega-resorts. She often travels during off-peak seasons to ensure maximum privacy.

Friend Group Getaways

Known for traveling with close friends including Courteney Cox and Jimmy Kimmel. Her birthday trips are legendary multi-day celebrations.

Signature Destinations

Cabo San Lucas, Mexico

Tier A Verified

Aniston's most frequented vacation spot. She's been photographed in Cabo dozens of times, often staying at ultra-private villa compounds in the Palmilla area. Key visits include her 50th birthday celebration in 2019, which brought together A-list friends for a multi-day beach party.

Preferred Properties:

  • One&Only Palmilla — Her go-to resort with private beach access
  • Las Ventanas al Paraíso — Rosewood property known for butler service
  • Private Villa Rentals — Often books $10,000+/night compounds

Evidence: Multiple paparazzi photos, Instagram friend tags, confirmed 50th birthday location

Santorini & Greek Islands

Tier A Verified

Aniston's Greek heritage draws her back to the islands regularly. She's been spotted multiple times in Santorini, enjoying the iconic sunsets and Mediterranean cuisine. Also known to visit Crete for its wellness retreats.

Highlights:

  • Oia sunset views from private villa terraces
  • Traditional Greek cooking experiences
  • Yacht excursions around the caldera

Bora Bora, French Polynesia

Tier A Verified

Visited for her honeymoon with Justin Theroux in 2015. The couple stayed at the Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora in an overwater bungalow. She's returned since for wellness retreats.

Tier B Confirmed

The bohemian-luxe Riviera Maya destination has become a favorite for Aniston's yoga retreats. She appreciates the area's focus on wellness, sustainable tourism, and spiritual experiences.

Big Sur, California

Tier B Confirmed

For domestic escapes, Aniston retreats to Big Sur's dramatic coastline. The Post Ranch Inn is her preferred property, offering cliffside rooms with ocean views and a world-renowned spa.

Travel Like Jennifer Aniston

Book the Right Room

Aniston always requests corner suites or standalone villas for maximum privacy. Ask about rooms away from main pathways and with private entrances.

Schedule Wellness First

Before booking flights, she confirms spa availability. Many top wellness resorts require weeks of advance booking for signature treatments.

Travel Off-Peak

She often visits popular destinations during shoulder seasons—fewer crowds, better service, and enhanced privacy.

Bring Your Circle

Group travel with trusted friends has become her signature. Many resorts offer multi-villa compounds perfect for friend getaways.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Jennifer Aniston vacation most often?

Cabo San Lucas, Mexico is Jennifer Aniston's most-visited vacation destination. She's been photographed there numerous times, including for her 50th and 55th birthday celebrations. She particularly favors the One&Only Palmilla resort.

Where did Jennifer Aniston honeymoon?

Jennifer Aniston and Justin Theroux honeymooned at the Four Seasons Resort Bora Bora in French Polynesia in August 2015. They stayed for approximately three weeks in an overwater bungalow.

Does Jennifer Aniston travel to Greece?

Yes, Aniston frequently visits Greece, particularly Santorini and the Greek Islands. Her Greek heritage draws her to the country, and she's been spotted enjoying yacht trips through the islands and staying in luxury villas in Oia.

What type of hotels does Jennifer Aniston prefer?

Aniston prefers boutique luxury properties with exceptional spa facilities and strong privacy. She gravitates toward private villas over hotel rooms and often books entire floors or standalone accommodations.
