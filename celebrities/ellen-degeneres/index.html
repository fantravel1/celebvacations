Travel Profile

Ellen DeGeneres ended The Ellen DeGeneres Show in 2022, freeing her schedule for travel. She's famous for real estate—buying, renovating, and selling luxury properties in the Montecito area, making home itself a form of travel.

African safaris are a passion she shares with wife Portia de Rossi—she's documented multiple trips to Rwanda to see gorillas. In 2024, reports indicated she and Portia purchased property in the UK, signaling potential international relocation.

Born in Metairie, Louisiana, she started comedy in New Orleans before moving to Los Angeles for her career. Her love of animals influences travel choices, often involving wildlife experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Ellen DeGeneres live?

Ellen DeGeneres has been based in the Montecito/Santa Barbara area of California for years, though reports in 2024 indicated she and wife Portia de Rossi purchased property in the Cotswolds, UK. She's famous for buying and selling numerous luxury properties.

Where is Ellen DeGeneres from?

Ellen DeGeneres was born in Metairie, Louisiana (a suburb of New Orleans). She started her comedy career in New Orleans before moving to Los Angeles. Her Louisiana roots appear occasionally in her comedy and personal stories.
