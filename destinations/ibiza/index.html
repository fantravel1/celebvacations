About Ibiza

Ibiza holds an almost mythical status in celebrity culture. The Spanish island has reinvented itself from hippie haven to superclub capital to luxury wellness destination—and celebrities have followed each evolution.

Today's Ibiza offers multiple experiences. The club scene still draws A-list DJs and partygoing celebrities, with Pacha, Amnesia, and Ushuaia hosting headline acts all summer. But increasingly, celebrities discover the island's quieter side: farm-to-table restaurants in the hills, yoga retreats, crystal-clear swimming coves, and white-washed villages that retain their bohemian charm.

The yacht culture around Ibiza has exploded, with Port Ibiza and marinas hosting superyachts all summer. Celebrities often anchor offshore, tendering into beach clubs by day and nightclubs by evening. For many, the yacht provides the perfect base—private sanctuary with easy access to the island's offerings.
