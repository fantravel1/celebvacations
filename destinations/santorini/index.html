Santorini

Where Volcanic Drama Meets Eternal Romance

Cyclades, Greece World's Most Romantic Island
#1 Sunset on Earth
€800+ Avg Hotel/Night
Jun-Sep Peak Season

Santorini needs no introduction, yet it continues to exceed every expectation. This crescent-shaped volcanic island in the Aegean Sea has been captivating visitors since ancient times—and capturing celebrity hearts since the jet-set era. The caldera, created by one of history's largest volcanic eruptions, provides the world's most dramatic natural backdrop.

For celebrities, Santorini offers something increasingly rare: a setting so inherently beautiful that no staging is required. The white-washed villages of Oia and Fira, cascading down cliff faces toward impossibly blue water, create a visual experience that even Hollywood can't replicate. It's where proposals happen, honeymoons unfold, and milestone birthdays are celebrated.

Village Guide

Oia

Celebrity Epicenter

The northern tip village is Santorini's crown jewel. The famous sunset, watched from the Byzantine castle ruins, attracts hundreds nightly—but celebrities access private hotel terraces with unobstructed views. Narrow cobblestone paths wind past galleries, boutiques, and cave restaurants. Every turn reveals a photograph worth framing.

Oia Highlights:

  • Castle Sunset: The world-famous viewpoint (arrive 2 hours early)
  • Ammoudi Bay: Tiny harbor with exceptional fish tavernas
  • Art Galleries: Some of Greece's finest contemporary art
  • Shopping: Jewelry, fashion, and artisan crafts

Fira

Capital

The island's main town, perched on the caldera rim. More energy and nightlife than Oia, with excellent restaurants, bars, and the Archaeological Museum. Cable car rides to the old port below.

Imerovigli

Highest Point

The "balcony to the Aegean"—the island's highest caldera village. Quieter than Fira or Oia, with stunning views and the Skaros Rock hike. Grace Santorini hotel is here.

Pyrgos

Medieval Charm

Inland hilltop village with Venetian castle ruins. More authentic and less touristy. Franco's Café offers perhaps the island's most beautiful sunset view away from the caldera.

Honeymoon Destination

Top Choice

Countless celebrity couples have honeymooned here. The combination of privacy, beauty, and romance makes it the default luxury honeymoon destination.

Dining

Lycabettus

Oia

Fine dining on the caldera's edge. Tasting menus featuring Aegean seafood and local produce. The sunset table is the most coveted seat on the island.

Ammoudi Fish Tavernas

Below Oia

Tiny harbor restaurants serving the freshest fish. Climb 300 steps down (or take a donkey) for grilled octopus and sea bream at water's edge. Genuine and unforgettable.

Selene

Fira

Pioneering modern Greek cuisine since 1986. Chef Yiannis Argyros elevates Cycladic ingredients to fine dining heights. Cooking classes also available.

Wine Experiences

Santo Wines

Cooperative winery with the island's most dramatic tasting terrace. Assyrtiko wines paired with caldera views. Reserve a private tasting for the full experience.

Venetsanos Winery

Restored 1947 winery built into the cliff face. Exceptional wines, stunning architecture, and sunset views that rival Oia's. The island's most sophisticated wine experience.

Planning Your Visit

Getting There

  • Fly: Santorini Airport (JTR), direct from Athens (45 min) and European cities in summer
  • Ferry: From Athens Piraeus port (5-8 hours) or high-speed ferry (4.5 hours)
  • Private: Charter flights and yacht arrivals common for celebrities
  • Island Transfer: Hotels arrange private transfers from airport/port

Best Times to Visit

  • June & September: Best balance—great weather, fewer crowds
  • July-August: Peak season, hottest, most crowded, highest prices
  • October: Still warm, dramatically fewer tourists
  • April-May: Shoulder season, some closures but beautiful

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Santorini good for a honeymoon?

Santorini is considered one of the world's top honeymoon destinations. The combination of dramatic caldera views, luxury cave hotels with private pools, legendary sunsets, and romantic dining creates an unmatched honeymoon atmosphere. Book caldera-facing suites with private pools for the ultimate experience.

Which celebrities have visited Santorini?

Eva Longoria, Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom, Kourtney Kardashian, Zac Efron, Jamie Foxx, and many others have documented Santorini visits. The island is particularly popular for celebrity honeymoons and milestone celebrations.

Oia vs Fira: which is better?

Oia is more picturesque, romantic, and exclusive—ideal for honeymooners and those seeking luxury. Fira is more lively with better nightlife, shopping, and central location. Most celebrities stay in Oia or Imerovigli for the views and privacy.
