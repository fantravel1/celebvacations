About Aspen

Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, Aspen has been the winter playground of choice for Hollywood's elite since the 1950s. What started as a quiet mining town has evolved into the world's most glamorous ski destination.

Aspen offers four world-class ski mountains—Aspen Mountain (Ajax), Aspen Highlands, Buttermilk, and Snowmass—providing terrain for everyone from beginners to expert skiers. But the appeal extends far beyond the slopes. The town's Victorian architecture, Michelin-worthy restaurants, and exclusive boutiques create an atmosphere of refined mountain luxury.

The celebrity presence is so significant that locals barely bat an eye at A-list sightings. From Mariah Carey's legendary annual Christmas visits to the Kardashians documenting their ski trips, Aspen has become synonymous with celebrity winter escapes. Many stars own multi-million dollar homes in the area, transforming seasonal visits into second-home living.

Why Celebrities Love Aspen

  • World-class skiing across four mountains with 5,500+ acres of terrain
  • Privacy and security in an established luxury community
  • Exceptional dining scene with celebrity chef restaurants
  • High-end shopping along the charming downtown core
  • Proximity to private jet facilities at Aspen/Pitkin County Airport
  • Summer appeal with hiking, music festivals, and cultural events
  • Established social scene with fellow celebrities and industry leaders

The Aspen Social Scene

Aspen's social calendar is packed with events that draw celebrities throughout the year. The winter season peaks during Christmas and New Year's when the town transforms into a snow-covered celebrity enclave. The annual X Games bring action sports stars and their famous fans, while summer brings the prestigious Aspen Ideas Festival and Aspen Music Festival, attracting thought leaders and classical music enthusiasts alike.

Best Time to Visit Aspen

Aspen offers year-round appeal, but celebrity activity varies significantly by season.

Peak Season: December - February

Ultimate celebrity season. Christmas through New Year's is the most star-studded time, with A-listers filling the slopes and restaurants. The X Games in late January brings another celebrity surge. Book accommodations months in advance and expect premium pricing.

Spring Skiing: March - April

Excellent conditions, fewer crowds. Longer days, warmer temperatures, and great snow conditions. Celebrity presence remains strong, with more relaxed atmosphere. Pool parties and rooftop scenes emerge.

Summer: June - August

Cultural renaissance. The Aspen Ideas Festival and Aspen Music Festival draw intellectual and artistic celebrities. Hiking, biking, and fly fishing replace skiing. A different but equally impressive celebrity crowd.

Fall Foliage: September - October

Shoulder season gem. Stunning golden aspens, Food & Wine Classic events, and the quietest celebrity scene. Perfect for those seeking the Aspen experience without the crowds.

Frequently Asked Questions About Aspen

Aspen attracts numerous celebrities including Mariah Carey (famous for her annual Christmas visits), Kate Hudson, Goldie Hawn & Kurt Russell, Paris Hilton, the Kardashian-Jenner family, Antonio Banderas, Heidi Klum, Seal, Kevin Costner, and many more. The town has been a celebrity haven since the 1950s, with many A-listers owning property in exclusive neighborhoods like Red Mountain, Starwood, and the West End.

Peak celebrity season runs from late December through early March, with Christmas and New Year's being the most star-studded period. The X Games in late January brings action sports celebrities and their famous fans. Summer also draws celebrities for the Aspen Ideas Festival (late June) and Aspen Music Festival (July-August), though the crowd is more intellectual and artistic than Hollywood-focused.

The Little Nell is the most celebrity-favored hotel, offering ski-in/ski-out luxury and legendary service. The St. Regis Aspen Resort provides refined elegance with butler service. Many celebrities prefer to rent or own private homes in exclusive areas like Red Mountain (where multi-million dollar estates offer complete privacy) or the historic West End within walking distance of downtown.

Matsuhisa (Nobu's mountain location) is the definitive celebrity dining destination. Casa Tua offers Italian elegance with a private club feel. For après-ski, Ajax Tavern at The Little Nell is legendary. The Caribou Club is an exclusive members-only venue where celebrities socialize away from public view. Element 47 offers fine dining, while Cache Cache and Piñons provide elevated American cuisine.
