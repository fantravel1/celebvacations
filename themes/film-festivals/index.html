Celebrities at Film Festivals

Red carpet glamour from Cannes to Sundance: Where stars stay, what they wear, and how to experience festival culture.

Where Do Celebrities Stay During Film Festivals?

Quick Answer: At Cannes, celebrities stay at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc (the ultimate choice), Hotel Martinez, and Carlton InterContinental, or aboard superyachts in the harbor. Venice stars choose the Belmond Hotel Cipriani and Aman Venice. Sundance celebrities stay at private homes in Deer Valley and luxury condos in Park City, while TIFF sees stars at the Ritz-Carlton Toronto and Four Seasons.

The Most Glamorous

Cannes Film Festival

May on the French Riviera brings Hollywood's biggest stars to the Croisette

Celebrity Hotels in Cannes

Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc

The undisputed champion of Cannes glamour. Located in Antibes, this legendary hotel hosts the annual amfAR gala and is home to stars like Leonardo DiCaprio, Brad Pitt, and Sharon Stone. Rooms start at $1,500/night during festival.

Most Exclusive

Hotel Martinez

Art Deco masterpiece on the Croisette with the largest private beach. Julia Roberts, Cate Blanchett, and George Clooney have stayed here. Home to the Martinez Suite with private rooftop terrace.

Carlton InterContinental

Historic Belle Époque landmark where Grace Kelly met Prince Rainier. Featured in Hitchcock's "To Catch a Thief." Celebrity guests include Sofia Coppola and Quentin Tarantino.

Cannes Celebrity Scene

The Cannes Film Festival (Festival de Cannes) runs for approximately 12 days in May. Key celebrity moments include:

  • Opening Night: A-list premiere with red carpet arrivals at the Palais des Festivals
  • amfAR Gala: Exclusive charity event at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc with performances and auction
  • Daily Premieres: Red carpet events with strict dress code (black tie for evening)
  • Yacht Parties: Exclusive events on superyachts in the harbor
  • Beach Clubs: Celebrities spotted at Martinez Beach and private beach clubs
Old World Glamour

Venice Film Festival

Late August brings celebrity arrivals via water taxi to the Lido

Where Celebrities Stay in Venice

Belmond Hotel Cipriani on Giudecca Island is the premier choice, accessible only by private boat. George Clooney, who married Amal in Venice, is a regular. The hotel offers ultimate privacy with gardens and a pool.

Aman Venice in a 16th-century palazzo on the Grand Canal hosts stars seeking discretion. Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie stayed here before their wedding nearby.

The Gritti Palace on the Grand Canal is favored by European royalty and Hollywood veterans. Historic rooms overlook the water.

During the festival, many celebrities stay at the Excelsior Venice Lido Resort, directly adjacent to the festival venue, for convenience.

Venice Festival Highlights

  • Water Taxi Arrivals: Iconic celebrity arrivals by boat to the Lido
  • Palazzo Parties: Private events in historic Venetian palaces
  • Red Carpet: Evening premieres at the Palazzo del Cinema
  • Golden Lion: Prestigious award ceremony
  • Extended Stays: Many celebrities explore Venice, Murano, and Burano

Venice runs late August to early September, often seen as the kickoff to Oscar season with major studio releases premiering here.

Independent Spirit

Sundance Film Festival

January in Park City brings celebrities to the mountains of Utah

Celebrity Accommodations at Sundance

Unlike Cannes and Venice, Sundance has limited hotel options. Celebrities typically stay in:

Private Homes in Deer Valley

Most A-listers rent luxury homes in Deer Valley, offering privacy, space for entourages, and ski-in/ski-out access. Robert Redford, the festival's founder, sets the tone for this mountain retreat approach.

Montage Deer Valley

The area's most luxurious hotel, offering ski access and spa. Popular with celebrities who prefer hotel amenities. About 15 minutes from Main Street.

Stein Eriksen Lodge

Scandinavian-style luxury lodge in Deer Valley. Named after Olympic gold medalist. Known for excellent service and privacy.

Park City Condos

Production companies often book luxury condos on Main Street for talent, providing walking access to theaters and events.

The Sundance Scene

Sundance is known for its more casual, accessible vibe compared to European festivals. Celebrities walk Main Street, duck into coffee shops, and attend parties at venues like:

  • Main Street: The heart of the festival with theaters, lounges, and celebrity sightings
  • Brand Lounges: Acura, Chase, and other sponsors host gifting suites
  • Filmmaker Lodge: Official gathering spot for industry and talent
  • Night Parties: TAO, WME, and studio parties in various venues
  • Skiing: Park City Mountain and Deer Valley attract celebrity skiers
Major Festival Circuit

TIFF & Berlin

Toronto International Film Festival (TIFF)

September in Toronto has become crucial for Oscar campaigns. Celebrity hotels include:

  • Ritz-Carlton Toronto: Steps from the festival hub, favored by A-listers
  • Four Seasons Toronto: Yorkville location, luxury choice
  • Shangri-La Toronto: Modern luxury, popular with studios
  • Thompson Toronto: Trendy option with rooftop bar

TIFF is known as the "people's festival" with public screenings and accessible celebrity Q&As. Many stars walk King Street and dine in the Entertainment District.

Berlin International Film Festival

February's Berlinale is Europe's largest public film festival. Celebrity stays include:

  • Hotel Adlon Kempinski: Historic hotel at Brandenburg Gate
  • The Regent Berlin: Intimate luxury near Gendarmenmarkt
  • Soho House Berlin: Creative crowd favorite
  • Hotel de Rome: Rocco Forte property in former bank

Berlin is known for its artistic focus and political cinema. The festival spans across Potsdamer Platz with multiple venues.

Common Questions

Film Festival FAQs

Where do celebrities stay during Cannes Film Festival?

During Cannes Film Festival, A-list celebrities stay at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc in Antibes, the Martinez, Carlton InterContinental, and Hotel Majestic Barrière. Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc is the most exclusive choice, hosting stars like Leonardo DiCaprio, Brad Pitt, and Julia Roberts. Many celebrities also rent private villas along the French Riviera or anchor superyachts in the harbor.

Which film festivals attract the most celebrities?

The Cannes Film Festival attracts the most celebrities, followed by Venice Film Festival, Toronto International Film Festival (TIFF), Sundance Film Festival, and Berlin International Film Festival. Cannes and Venice are known for European glamour, while Sundance focuses on independent cinema and TIFF has become crucial for Oscar campaigns.

What do celebrities wear to film festivals?

At Cannes and Venice, celebrities wear haute couture gowns and custom-designed pieces by Chanel, Dior, Valentino, and Armani. Black tie is required for evening premieres. Sundance is more casual, with celebrities wearing designer winter wear from brands like Moncler and Canada Goose. TIFF strikes a balance between Hollywood glamour and accessible fashion.

Can regular people attend film festivals with celebrities?

Yes, but access varies. Sundance is the most accessible, with passes available for purchase. Cannes and Venice require accreditation but offer public screenings. The prestigious red carpet premieres are invitation-only. Festival towns like Park City and Cannes have public areas where celebrity sightings are common at restaurants, parties, and walking between venues.

How long do celebrities stay at film festivals?

Most celebrities stay 2-5 days at film festivals, timed around their film's premiere. Major stars may attend opening and closing ceremonies, staying up to 12 days at Cannes. Some extend their stay for vacation, especially at Cannes where the French Riviera beckons, or Venice with its romantic appeal.
