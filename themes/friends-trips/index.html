Celebrity Friends Trips

Squad goals realized: Where celebrity friend groups vacation together, from Mediterranean yachts to Coachella compounds.

Where Do Celebrity Friend Groups Vacation?

Quick Answer: Celebrity squads favor yacht charters in the Mediterranean and Caribbean, villa rentals in destinations like St. Barts, Cabo, and Tuscany, and festival trips to Coachella and Art Basel. Taylor Swift's squad, the Kardashian-Jenner crew, and Leonardo DiCaprio's group are among the most documented friend travel circles. Birthday trips and bachelorettes drive many celebrity group vacations.

Squad Goals

Famous Celebrity Friend Groups

Taylor Swift's Squad

The most documented celebrity friend group of the 2010s. Includes Selena Gomez, Gigi and Bella Hadid, Blake Lively, and rotating members. Famous for Fourth of July parties in Rhode Island and coordinated festival appearances. Travel includes beach house rentals, NYC hangouts, and international shows.

Music/Fashion

Kardashian-Jenner Circle

The famous family plus friends like Malika Haqq, Stassie Karanikolaou, and Sofia Richie. Known for birthday trips to exotic destinations, matching outfits on vacation, and content-creating travel. Favorite spots include Turks & Caicos, Aspen, and Cabo.

Reality/Business

Leonardo DiCaprio's Crew

Long-running friend group including Tobey Maguire, Lukas Haas, and Kevin Connolly—dubbed the "P*ssy Posse" in the '90s. Known for yacht trips, Super Bowl appearances, and St. Barts New Year's. Privacy-focused but regularly photographed.

Film

British Celebrity Crew

David and Victoria Beckham, Gordon Ramsay, Elton John and David Furnish form a loose social circle. Known for South of France summers, English countryside weekends, and family holidays. Cross-generational friendships.

Celebrity Girls Trip Destinations

Cabo San Lucas

The ultimate bachelorette and birthday destination. Beach clubs, villa rentals, private yacht days. Easy LA access makes it convenient. Kourtney Kardashian, Jennifer Aniston have celebrated here.

Mexico

Tulum

Bohemian beach meets wellness. Jungle cenotes, beach clubs, spiritual retreats. Popular with model and fashion crowds. Instagram-perfect aesthetics. Wellness-focused girls trips.

Mexico

St. Barts

Luxury beach escape for high-profile groups. Villa rentals on hillsides, yacht days, St. Jean Beach. New Year's and winter holidays. Expensive but exclusive.

Caribbean

Ibiza

Party island for birthday celebrations. Beach clubs, mega-clubs, yacht parties. European celebrities and models favor summer visits. Combine partying with villa relaxation.

Spain

Miraval Arizona

Wellness spa resort for fitness and self-care focused trips. Healthy cuisine, fitness classes, desert beauty. Celebrities seeking relaxation over partying.

Arizona

Greek Islands

Mykonos for parties, Santorini for photos, smaller islands for privacy. Yacht-hopping perfect for groups. Summer season brings celebrity sightings daily.

Ski Trips

Common Questions

Friends Trip FAQs

Which celebrity friend groups travel together?

Famous celebrity friend groups who travel together include Taylor Swift's squad, the Kardashian-Jenner family and their friends, Leonardo DiCaprio's crew, and various model friend groups. British celebrities like David Beckham and Guy Ritchie vacation together.

Where do celebrities go for girls trips?

Popular celebrity girls trip destinations include Cabo San Lucas (bachelorettes), Tulum (wellness and beach), St. Barts (luxury beach), Ibiza (party scene), and spa destinations like Miraval Arizona. Yacht trips in the Mediterranean are also favorites.

Do celebrities rent yachts for group vacations?

Yes, yacht charters are extremely popular for celebrity friend trips. Groups charter superyachts in the Mediterranean, Caribbean, and Greek islands. Charter rates range from $100,000 to over $1 million per week.

Where do celebrities go for guys trips?

Celebrity guys trips often involve golf destinations (Pebble Beach, Scottsdale), Las Vegas for events, fishing trips (Costa Rica, Cabo), ski trips (Aspen, Deer Valley), and sporting events.
