Celebrity Wine Country Vacations

From Napa Valley estates to Tuscan vineyards: Where celebrities own wineries, taste rare vintages, and escape to rolling hills.

Which Celebrities Own Wineries?

Quick Answer: Major celebrity winery owners include Francis Ford Coppola (Inglenook, Napa), Brad Pitt (Château Miraval, Provence), Sting (Il Palagio, Tuscany), Dave Matthews (Dreaming Tree), and Cameron Diaz (Avaline wine brand). Many celebrities invest in wine labels or own vineyard estates without producing commercially, using the properties as private retreats.

America's Wine Capital

Napa Valley

California's premier wine region draws celebrities for its world-class wines and luxury lifestyle

Celebrity Wineries in Napa

Inglenook (Francis Ford Coppola)

The legendary director owns this historic Rutherford estate, home of the original Rubicon wine. Restored to glory, it produces acclaimed Cabernet Sauvignon. Open for tours and tastings in a stunning château setting.

Must Visit

Cliff Lede Vineyards

Canadian entertainment mogul Cliff Lede created a rock 'n' roll-themed winery in Stags Leap. Guitar-shaped gardens and music-inspired wine names. The adjacent Poetry Inn is ultra-exclusive.

Andretti Winery

Racing legend Mario Andretti's winery produces Italian varieties reflecting his heritage. The Tuscan-style winery in Oak Knoll offers tastings and bocce courts.

Staglin Family Vineyard

While not celebrity-owned, this Rutherford estate hosts the legendary Music Festival for Brain Health, bringing celebrities and tech moguls together for charity.

Where Celebrities Stay in Napa

Meadowood Napa Valley

The most exclusive resort in Napa. Croquet lawns, Michelin three-star Restaurant (The Restaurant at Meadowood). Private cottages hidden in forest. Celebrity weddings and retreats.

Most Exclusive

Auberge du Soleil

Hillside luxury with panoramic valley views. Mediterranean-style villas with private decks. Restaurant is a Napa classic. Celebrity favorite for anniversary trips.

Las Alcobas St. Helena

Boutique luxury on Main Street. Walking distance to restaurants and shops. Modern design with vineyard views. Acacia House restaurant on property.

The Napa Celebrity Experience

  • The French Laundry: Thomas Keller's three-star restaurant requires reservations months ahead—celebrities often get special treatment
  • Cult Wine Tastings: Screaming Eagle, Harlan, and Scarecrow by appointment only (and often waiting lists)
  • Hot Air Balloons: Sunrise balloon rides over vineyards, champagne breakfast included
  • Auction Napa Valley: June charity event brings billionaires and celebrities together
  • BottleRock Festival: May music festival in Napa draws celebrity performers and attendees
French Wine Country

Provence & Bordeaux

Hollywood's love affair with French wine estates

Château Miraval (Brad Pitt)

The most famous celebrity winery in the world. Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie purchased this 1,000-acre Provence estate in 2012 for $60 million. They married here in 2014.

Miraval produces acclaimed Miraval Rosé in partnership with the Perrin family (Château de Beaucastel). The wine has become one of Provence's most sought-after rosés.

The estate includes a recording studio where Pink Floyd recorded parts of "The Wall." The château itself is 17th century with extensive gardens. Post-divorce, Brad Pitt retained involvement in the wine business.

Not open to public tours, but the wine is widely available.

Provence Celebrity Scene

George Lucas owns Château Margüi in Provence, producing rosé and red wines from his estate in the Var region.

Celebrities vacation throughout Provence:

  • Saint-Tropez: Beach clubs and rosé culture draw celebrities each summer
  • Gordes & Luberon: Hilltop villages popular with celebrities seeking privacy
  • Aix-en-Provence: Cultural hub with excellent restaurants and wine bars
  • Châteauneuf-du-Pape: Historic wine region for serious collectors

Where to Stay: La Bastide de Gordes, Hôtel Crillon le Brave, Les Baux de Provence for celebrity-level luxury.

Bordeaux

Bordeaux attracts wealthy collectors and investors rather than Hollywood celebrities. However, the region offers magnificent château experiences:

Les Sources de Caudalie

Spa hotel in Bordeaux vineyards offering vinotherapy treatments. Adjacent to Château Smith Haut Lafitte. Celebrity wellness destination.

La Grande Maison

Boutique hotel in Bordeaux city with two-Michelin-star restaurant by Pierre Gagnaire. Intimate luxury for serious wine enthusiasts.

First Growth Tours

Private visits to Margaux, Latour, Haut-Brion, and Mouton Rothschild. Requires connections and advance booking. The ultimate wine experience.

Italian Wine Country

Tuscany

Rolling hills, ancient villas, and Super Tuscan wines

Il Palagio (Sting)

Sting and Trudie Styler's Tuscan estate has been their home since 1999. Located near Figline Valdarno, the 16th-century villa produces organic wines and olive oil.

The estate makes Chianti-style reds, whites, and rosé under the "Il Palagio" and "Message in a Bottle" labels—the latter named after the Police song.

Sting hosts occasional concerts at the estate and the wines are available internationally. The couple practices biodynamic farming.

Tuscan Celebrity Destinations

  • Montalcino: Home of Brunello, Italy's most prestigious red wine
  • Bolgheri: Super Tuscan territory (Sassicaia, Ornellaia)
  • Chianti Classico: Classic rolling hills and medieval villages
  • Montepulciano: Renaissance architecture and Vino Nobile
  • Cortona: "Under the Tuscan Sun" fame

Where to Stay: Castello di Casole, Rosewood Castiglion del Bosco, Borgo San Felice.

Common Questions

Wine Country FAQs

Which celebrities own wineries in Napa Valley?

Celebrities with Napa Valley wineries include Francis Ford Coppola (Inglenook), Dave Matthews (Dreaming Tree), Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson (Teremana-adjacent), Boz Scaggs, and Mario Andretti. Many celebrities also invest in wine brands: The Prisoner Wine Company was backed by tech billionaires, and numerous athletes have Napa connections.

Where do celebrities stay in Napa Valley?

Celebrities stay at Meadowood Napa Valley (exclusive resort with Michelin three-star restaurant), Auberge du Soleil (hillside luxury with views), Calistoga Ranch, and Las Alcobas St. Helena. For privacy, many rent estate homes in Oakville, Yountville, or Calistoga. The French Laundry in Yountville draws celebrities for its legendary dining.

Which celebrities have Provence or French wine estates?

Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie (formerly together) own Château Miraval in Provence, producing acclaimed rosé. George Lucas owns Château Margüi in Provence. French celebrities and international stars are drawn to Provence for rosé production and the lifestyle. Bordeaux châteaux attract Chinese billionaires and finance figures more than Hollywood.

Do any celebrities own Italian wineries?

Sting owns Il Palagio estate in Tuscany producing organic wines. Andrea Bocelli's family has long produced wine in Tuscany. Various international celebrities own Tuscan properties with vineyards. George Clooney's Lake Como presence has sparked interest in northern Italian wines, though he's not a winery owner.

What is celebrity wine tourism like?

Celebrity wine tourism combines private vineyard tours, exclusive tastings, farm-to-table dining, and luxury accommodations. In Napa, celebrities visit cult wineries like Screaming Eagle and Harlan by appointment only. Hot air balloon rides over vineyards, private chef experiences, and spa treatments complete the wine country experience.
