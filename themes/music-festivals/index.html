Celebrities at Music Festivals

From the desert to the beach: Coachella, Art Basel, Glastonbury and where the world's biggest stars party.

Where Do Celebrities Stay During Coachella?

Quick Answer: A-list celebrities rent private estates in La Quinta and Indian Wells with pools, security, and desert views. Hotels include the Parker Palm Springs ($1,500+/night during festival), La Quinta Resort, and Ritz-Carlton Rancho Mirage. Some celebrities stay in luxury safari tents in the on-site VIP camping area with air conditioning and private bathrooms.

The Fashion Festival

Coachella Valley Music & Arts Festival

April in the California desert draws the biggest names in music, fashion, and entertainment

Celebrity Accommodations at Coachella

Private Estate Rentals

Most A-listers rent mansions in La Quinta, Indian Wells, and Rancho Mirage. Properties feature pools, guest houses, and security gates. Beyoncé and other headliners stay in these private compounds. Rentals range from $10,000 to $100,000+ for the weekend.

Most Popular

The Parker Palm Springs

The most fashionable hotel choice, known for its Jonathan Adler design and celebrity-magnet pool scene. Leonardo DiCaprio, Kendall Jenner, and Paris Hilton have stayed here. About 25 minutes from the festival.

Safari Camping VIP

On-site luxury tents with real beds, air conditioning, private bathrooms, and concierge service. Perfect for celebrities who want the festival experience without roughing it. Includes VIP festival access.

La Quinta Resort & Club

Spanish-style resort with private casitas and 41 pools. Close to the festival grounds and popular with music industry professionals. Historic property dating to 1926.

The Coachella Celebrity Scene

  • Revolve Festival: The most exclusive Coachella party with performances and A-list guest list
  • Neon Carnival: Late-night party with carnival rides and celebrity DJ sets
  • Brand Houses: Poppy, American Express, and BMW host invite-only experiences
  • Artist Compound: Backstage area where performers and celebrity guests mingle
  • Rose Garden VIP: Premium viewing areas and exclusive lounges

Art Meets Party

Art Basel Miami Beach

December in Miami combines contemporary art with legendary parties

Where Celebrities Stay at Art Basel

Faena Hotel Miami Beach is the epicenter of Art Basel parties, with the famous Faena Forum hosting major art installations and events. Celebrities love the theatrical design and exclusive beach club.

The Setai Miami Beach offers Asian-inspired luxury with three infinity pools. Popular with celebrities seeking refinement over scene.

The Edition Miami Beach by Ian Schrager combines nightlife (Basement club) with five-star service. Bowling alley, ice rink, and celebrity DJ sets.

Soho Beach House provides members-only exclusivity with rooftop pool and ocean views. The go-to for creative industry celebrities.

Art Basel Celebrity Events

  • Miami Beach Convention Center: Main fair with premier galleries
  • Rubell Museum: Private collection with exclusive openings
  • Design District Parties: Brand activations and gallery openings
  • Yacht Events: Private parties in Miami Beach Marina
  • Wynwood: Street art district with warehouse parties
  • Faena Forum: Performance art and celebrity-filled dinners

Art Basel Miami runs for one week in early December, transforming Miami Beach into a global art and social scene.

British Institution

Glastonbury Festival

June in Somerset, England for the world's most famous music festival

Celebrity Experience at Glastonbury

Unlike American festivals, Glastonbury maintains its counter-culture roots even for celebrities:

Glamping & VIP Areas

Celebrities stay in luxury "glamping" areas like Worthy View and Pop-Up Hotel, with proper beds, private bathrooms, and catering. The Backstage area offers artist-only access with its own bars and viewing areas.

Nearby Hotels

Babington House (Soho House property) is the celebrity favorite, about 30 minutes away. The Pig Near Bath offers countryside luxury. Some celebrities helicopter in from London.

Glastonbury Celebrity Sightings

British celebrities embrace the mud and chaos. Regular attendees include:

  • Kate Moss: Festival fashion icon, seen in wellies and vintage
  • Sienna Miller: Another boho pioneer at Glastonbury
  • Cara Delevingne: Model turned festival regular
  • Harry Styles: Both performer and attendee
  • Stella McCartney: Often backstage with her musician father
Festival Circuit

More Celebrity Festival Destinations

Lollapalooza Chicago

August in Grant Park. Celebrities stay at the Peninsula Chicago and Soho House Chicago. Known for hip-hop and alternative acts with a younger celebrity crowd.

Austin City Limits

October in Austin. Celebrities stay at the Austin Proper and Hotel Saint Cecilia. Texas hospitality meets music with BBQ and country vibes.

Governors Ball NYC

June in New York. Celebrities stay at their regular NYC hotels (The Mark, Greenwich Hotel). Urban festival with Manhattan skyline views.

Tomorrowland Belgium

July EDM festival. DJs are the celebrities here. Dreamville camping and nearby Antwerp hotels. Elaborate stage designs and global crowd.

Burning Man

August in Nevada desert. Celebrities camp in elaborate structures or luxury RVs. Tech billionaires and artists gather for this non-commercial event.

Primavera Sound

June in Barcelona. Celebrities stay in Gothic Quarter hotels. European indie festival with beach and city access.

Common Questions

Music Festival FAQs

Where do celebrities stay during Coachella?

During Coachella, celebrities stay at luxury estate rentals in La Quinta and Indian Wells, the Parker Palm Springs, La Quinta Resort, and the Ritz-Carlton Rancho Mirage. Many A-listers rent private mansions with pools and security in gated communities. Some celebrities camp in luxury 'glamping' safari tents on the festival grounds in the VIP camping area.

Which music festivals attract the most celebrities?

Coachella attracts the most celebrities, particularly for fashion and social media content. Art Basel Miami Beach combines art and music with celebrity appeal. Glastonbury in the UK attracts British celebrities and musicians. Other celebrity favorites include Lollapalooza Chicago, Austin City Limits, and the Governors Ball in New York.

What do celebrities wear to Coachella?

Coachella celebrity fashion includes boho-chic styles, designer festival wear from brands like Revolve, Cult Gaia, and For Love & Lemons, plus statement accessories like cowboy boots, fringe, and layered jewelry. Influencers and celebrities often wear sponsored outfits and attend brand parties. The festival has become a major fashion event rivaling fashion weeks.

Are there VIP areas at Coachella for celebrities?

Yes, Coachella has VIP and guest areas with exclusive viewing platforms, premium bathrooms, and catered food. The Safari Camping area offers luxury tents with concierge service. Celebrities also access brand-sponsored parties and private estates near the festival. Many brands like Revolve, Neon Carnival, and HP host exclusive invite-only events.

What is Art Basel Miami and why do celebrities attend?

Art Basel Miami Beach is an international art fair held every December in Miami, combining contemporary art exhibitions with exclusive parties, yacht events, and performances. Celebrities attend for the intersection of art, fashion, and nightlife. Events include the NADA art fair, Design Miami, and numerous parties at venues like the Faena Hotel and clubs in South Beach.
