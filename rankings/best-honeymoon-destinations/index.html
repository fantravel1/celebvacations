Best Celebrity Honeymoon Destinations

Where famous couples celebrate their newlywed bliss: From Maldives villas to private islands.

Where Do Celebrities Honeymoon?

Quick Answer: The Maldives dominates celebrity honeymoons with overwater villas and extreme privacy. Bora Bora remains the classic choice for French Polynesian romance. The Amalfi Coast offers European glamour, while Seychelles and Fiji provide private island seclusion. Key factors: privacy, luxury service, romantic settings, and Instagram-worthy backdrops.

For Newlyweds

Top 15 Celebrity Honeymoon Destinations

1

Maldives

The undisputed honeymoon champion. Overwater villas with glass floors, private pools, and butler service. Soneva Fushi, Cheval Blanc Randheli, One&Only Reethi Rah, and Waldorf Astoria are top choices. Complete privacy on private islands. Celebrity honeymooners include Tom Hiddleston, Priyanka Chopra & Nick Jonas.

#1 Honeymoon
2

Bora Bora, French Polynesia

The iconic overwater bungalow destination. Mount Otemanu backdrop for romantic photos. Four Seasons, Conrad, and St. Regis lead. Jennifer Aniston, Nicole Kidman, and countless celebrities have honeymooned here. Crystal lagoons and Polynesian culture.

Classic Romance
3

Amalfi Coast, Italy

European romance along dramatic cliffs. Positano, Ravello, and Capri offer celebrity-approved honeymoon settings. Belmond Hotel Caruso and Le Sirenuse are legendary. John Legend & Chrissy Teigen, Reese Witherspoon chose Italian honeymoons.

European Glamour
4

Seychelles

Private island honeymoons in the Indian Ocean. North Island (where William & Kate honeymooned), Fregate Island Private, and Four Seasons offer extreme seclusion. Giant tortoises, pristine beaches, and tropical luxury.

Royal Choice
5

Fiji

South Pacific paradise with private island options. Laucala Island (owned by Red Bull heir), Turtle Island, and Kokomo Private Island. Bula spirit hospitality. Remote yet accessible. Michelle Obama has vacationed.

Private Islands
6

Santorini, Greece

Iconic blue domes and sunset views. Canaves Oia and Grace Hotel for celebrity stays. Caldera views and wine tasting. More accessible than private islands but equally photogenic for honeymoon memories.

Iconic Views
7

Lake Como, Italy

George Clooney's Italian home brought attention to this romantic destination. Villa d'Este and Grand Hotel Tremezzo. Mountain-ringed lake, historic villas. Sophisticated European honeymoon alternative.

Clooney Approved
8

Tahiti & Moorea

French Polynesian alternatives to Bora Bora. The Brando on Tetiaroa (Marlon Brando's private island) is the ultimate eco-luxury honeymoon. Moorea's Sofitel and Hilton offer overwater bungalows at lower prices.

Eco-Luxury
9

Hawaii (Maui & Kauai)

Accessible luxury honeymoons. Four Seasons Maui and Wailea resorts for beach luxury. Kauai's St. Regis Princeville for dramatic cliffs. No passport required for Americans. Many celebrities own Hawaii homes.

Easy Access
10

Turks & Caicos

Caribbean honeymoon with world-class beaches. Grace Bay consistently ranked top beach globally. COMO Parrot Cay offers celebrity-level privacy on private island. Short flight from US East Coast.

Best Beaches
11

Paris, France

Classic city honeymoon for cultural couples. Ritz Paris, Plaza Athénée, Le Bristol. Museums, fine dining, romantic walks. Kim & Kanye's wedding celebration. Timeless honeymoon appeal.

City Romance
12

Maldives-adjacent: Sri Lanka

Combine Maldives beach time with Sri Lankan culture and safaris. Aman resorts in both countries. Honeymoon couples add elephant encounters and tea country to their trip.

Adventure Add-on
13

St. Barts

Caribbean glamour for honeymooners. Eden Rock and Cheval Blanc. French-Caribbean cuisine and beaches. More social scene than secluded island honeymoons but ultra-luxurious.

Caribbean Chic
14

Tuscany, Italy

Wine country honeymoons for food-loving couples. Rosewood Castiglion del Bosco and Castello di Casole. Vineyard stays, truffle hunting, cooking classes. Intimate villa honeymoons.

Wine & Romance
15

Japan

Cultural honeymoon for adventurous couples. Aman Tokyo and Kyoto, traditional ryokans. Cherry blossoms in spring. Combine ancient temples with modern luxury. Unique honeymoon alternative.

Cultural
Celebrity Couples

Famous Celebrity Honeymoons

Prince William & Kate

Honeymooned at North Island, Seychelles in 2011. The private island offers just 11 villas. Their visit put Seychelles firmly on the luxury honeymoon map.

George & Amal Clooney

Following their Venice wedding, honeymooned at their own Lake Como estate and the Seychelles. Combined Italian romance with tropical escape.

Priyanka & Nick Jonas

Honeymooned at Cheval Blanc in the Maldives following their multi-day Indian wedding celebrations. Shared stunning overwater villa photos.

Jennifer Aniston & Justin Theroux

Honeymooned in Bora Bora at a private island resort. Aniston famously loves the destination and has returned multiple times.

John Legend & Chrissy Teigen

Honeymooned in Italy along the Amalfi Coast following their Lake Como wedding. Combined cultural exploration with coastal relaxation.

Beyoncé & Jay-Z

Kept their honeymoon private, but reported to have visited multiple destinations including Seychelles and Mediterranean yacht charter.

Common Questions

Honeymoon FAQs

Where do celebrities go for their honeymoons?

The most popular celebrity honeymoon destinations are the Maldives (overwater villas with privacy), Bora Bora (French Polynesian paradise), the Amalfi Coast (Italian romance), Seychelles (private island luxury), and Fiji (South Pacific escape).

Which resorts do celebrities choose for honeymoons?

Top celebrity honeymoon resorts include Soneva Fushi and Cheval Blanc in the Maldives, Four Seasons Bora Bora, Belmond Hotel Caruso on the Amalfi Coast, North Island in Seychelles, and Laucala Island in Fiji.

How long do celebrity honeymoons last?

Celebrity honeymoons typically last 1-3 weeks, depending on work schedules. Some celebrities take multiple honeymoon trips over months. Extended honeymoons to distant destinations like the Maldives or Fiji are common for those with flexible schedules.
